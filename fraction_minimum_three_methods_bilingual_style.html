一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[ a>0,\quad b>0,\quad 2a+b=1 \]
\[ \text{求 }\frac{5a+b}{a^2+ab}\text{ 的最小值} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{9} \]
最小值为 \(9\)，当 \(a=b=\frac13\) 时取得。The minimum is \(9\), achieved when \(a=b=\frac13\).

三、方法一：代入化简，直接配方3. Method 1: Substitute and Complete the Square

由条件 \(2a+b=1\)，可得 \(b=1-2a\)。因为 \(a>0,b>0\)，所以 \(0From \(2a+b=1\), we get \(b=1-2a\). Since \(a>0,b>0\), \(0

\[ \frac{5a+b}{a^2+ab} =\frac{5a+(1-2a)}{a^2+a(1-2a)} =\frac{3a+1}{a-a^2} \]

证明它不小于 \(9\)：Prove it is at least \(9\):

\[ \frac{3a+1}{a-a^2}\ge 9 \]
\[ 3a+1\ge 9a-9a^2 \]
\[ 9a^2-6a+1\ge0 \]
\[ (3a-1)^2\ge0 \]
等号在 \(a=\frac13\) 时成立，此时 \(b=1-2a=\frac13\)。Equality holds when \(a=\frac13\). Then \(b=1-2a=\frac13\).

四、方法二：拆项 + 柯西不等式4. Method 2: Split the Fraction and Use Cauchy

先把分母写成 \(a(a+b)\)，再拆分分子：Write the denominator as \(a(a+b)\), then split the numerator:

\[ a^2+ab=a(a+b) \]
\[ 5a+b=(a+b)+4a \]
\[ \frac{5a+b}{a^2+ab} =\frac{(a+b)+4a}{a(a+b)} =\frac1a+\frac4{a+b} \]

用 Engel 形式的柯西不等式：Use the Engel form of Cauchy's inequality:

\[ \frac1a+\frac4{a+b} =\frac{1^2}{a}+\frac{2^2}{a+b} \ge \frac{(1+2)^2}{a+(a+b)} \]
\[ a+(a+b)=2a+b=1 \]
\[ \frac1a+\frac4{a+b}\ge 9 \]
等号条件为 \(\frac1a=\frac2{a+b}\)，即 \(a+b=2a\)，所以 \(b=a\)。再由 \(2a+b=1\)，得 \(a=b=\frac13\)。Equality requires \(\frac1a=\frac2{a+b}\), so \(a+b=2a\), hence \(b=a\). From \(2a+b=1\), \(a=b=\frac13\).

五、方法三：设参数 + 判别式5. Method 3: Parameter and Discriminant

设原式等于 \(k\)，并代入 \(b=1-2a\)：Let the expression equal \(k\), and substitute \(b=1-2a\):

\[ k=\frac{3a+1}{a-a^2} \]

整理成关于 \(a\) 的二次方程：Rearrange into a quadratic equation in \(a\):

\[ k(a-a^2)=3a+1 \]
\[ ka^2-(k-3)a+1=0 \]

因为存在实数 \(a\)，判别式必须满足：Since a real \(a\) exists, the discriminant must satisfy:

\[ \Delta=(k-3)^2-4k\ge0 \]
\[ k^2-10k+9\ge0 \]
\[ (k-1)(k-9)\ge0 \]
\[ k\le1\quad\text{或}\quad k\ge9 \]

但原式为正，且由拆项形式 \(\frac1a+\frac4{a+b}\) 可知不可能小于等于 \(1\)，所以只能 \(k\ge9\)。But the expression is positive, and from the split form \(\frac1a+\frac4{a+b}\), it cannot be at most \(1\). Thus \(k\ge9\).

所以最小值是 \(9\)。Therefore the minimum is \(9\).
