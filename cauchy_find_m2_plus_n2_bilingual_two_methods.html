三、方法一：柯西不等式3. Method 1: Cauchy's Inequality

令： Let:

\[ S=m^2+n^2 \]

把左边看成两个向量的点积： View the left side as the dot product of two vectors:

\[ m\sqrt{3-n^2}+n\sqrt{3-m^2} = (m,n)\cdot\left(\sqrt{3-n^2},\sqrt{3-m^2}\right) \]

由柯西不等式： By Cauchy's inequality:

\[ m\sqrt{3-n^2}+n\sqrt{3-m^2} \le \sqrt{m^2+n^2}\sqrt{(3-n^2)+(3-m^2)} \]
\[ 3\le \sqrt{S}\sqrt{6-S} \]

两边平方： Square both sides:

\[ 9\le S(6-S) \]
\[ S^2-6S+9\le0 \]
\[ (S-3)^2\le0 \]
平方数一定大于等于 \(0\)，所以只能 \((S-3)^2=0\)，即 \(S=3\)。 A square is always nonnegative, so \((S-3)^2\le0\) forces \((S-3)^2=0\), hence \(S=3\).

四、方法二：移项后平方，凑完全平方4. Method 2: Move One Term, Square, and Complete a Square

你提供的解法二：移项后平方 Your method 2: move one term and square

由原式： From the original equation:

\[ m\sqrt{3-n^2}+n\sqrt{3-m^2}=3 \]

移项： Move one term to the other side:

\[ m\sqrt{3-n^2}=3-n\sqrt{3-m^2} \]

两边平方： Square both sides:

\[ m^2(3-n^2) = \left(3-n\sqrt{3-m^2}\right)^2 \]
\[ 3m^2-m^2n^2 = 9-6n\sqrt{3-m^2}+n^2(3-m^2) \]

展开右边并消去共同项： Expand the right side and cancel common terms:

\[ 3m^2 = 9-6n\sqrt{3-m^2}+3n^2 \]

两边除以 \(3\)： Divide both sides by \(3\):

\[ m^2=3-2n\sqrt{3-m^2}+n^2 \]

整理： Rearrange:

\[ 3-m^2-2n\sqrt{3-m^2}+n^2=0 \]

这正好是一个完全平方： This is exactly a perfect square:

\[ \left(\sqrt{3-m^2}-n\right)^2=0 \]

所以： Therefore:

\[ \sqrt{3-m^2}=n \]

两边平方： Square both sides:

\[ 3-m^2=n^2 \]
\[ m^2+n^2=3 \]
所以答案为 \(\boxed{3}\)。 So the answer is \(\boxed{3}\).
