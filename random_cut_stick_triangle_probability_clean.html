一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：将一根木棍随机截成 3 段，能围成三角形的概率是多少？Original image: A stick is randomly cut into 3 pieces. What is the probability that the pieces can form a triangle?
\[ \text{将一根木棍随机截成 3 段，能围成三角形的概率是？} \]
通常理解为：在木棍上随机选两个切点，切成 3 段。The usual interpretation is: choose two random cut points on the stick and make 3 pieces.

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac{1}{4}=25\%} \]
所以选 B：25%。So the answer is B: 25%.

三、先表示三段长度3. Represent the Three Pieces

把木棍长度看作 \(1\)。随机取两个切点，设较小的切点为 \(x\)，较大的切点为 \(y\)，则：Take the stick length as \(1\). Choose two random cut points. Let the smaller cut be \(x\), the larger cut be \(y\). Then:

\[ 0\lt x\lt y\lt 1 \]

三段长度分别是：The three lengths are:

\[ x,\quad y-x,\quad 1-y \]

四、解法一：三角形条件4. Solution 1: Triangle Conditions

三段能围成三角形，等价于任意两段之和大于第三段。对三段 \(x,\ y-x,\ 1-y\)，得到：The pieces form a triangle iff the sum of any two sides is greater than the third. For \(x,\ y-x,\ 1-y\), we get:

\[ x+(y-x)\gt 1-y \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad y\gt\frac{1}{2} \]
\[ x+(1-y)\gt y-x \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad y-x\lt\frac{1}{2} \]
\[ (y-x)+(1-y)\gt x \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad x\lt\frac{1}{2} \]

所以需要同时满足：So we need all three:

\[ x\lt\frac{1}{2},\qquad y\gt\frac{1}{2},\qquad y\lt x+\frac{1}{2} \]

五、面积法求概率5. Area Method

总情况是三角形区域：The total sample space is the triangular region:

\[ 0\lt x\lt y\lt 1 \]
\[ \text{总面积}=\frac{1}{2} \]

能围成三角形的有利区域面积为：The favorable region has area:

\[ \int_0^{1/2}x\,dx=\frac{1}{8} \]

因此概率：Therefore the probability is:

\[ P=\frac{\frac{1}{8}}{\frac{1}{2}}=\frac{1}{4}=25\% \]
答案：25%Answer: 25%

六、解法二：最长一段必须小于一半6. Solution 2: The Longest Piece Must Be Less Than Half

三段能围成三角形，也等价于：最长的一段小于全长的一半。The pieces form a triangle iff the longest piece is less than half of the whole stick.

\[ \max(x,\ y-x,\ 1-y)\lt\frac{1}{2} \]

不成功的情况是：有一段长度大于 \(\frac{1}{2}\)。在三角形区域 \(0\lt x\lt y\lt 1\) 中，这三种坏情况面积相同，每块面积都是 \(\frac{1}{8}\)：Failure occurs when one piece is greater than \(\frac{1}{2}\). In the region \(0\lt x\lt y\lt 1\), the three bad regions have equal area, each \(\frac{1}{8}\):

\[ x\gt\frac{1}{2},\quad y-x\gt\frac{1}{2},\quad 1-y\gt\frac{1}{2} \]
\[ \text{坏区域总面积}=3\cdot\frac{1}{8}=\frac{3}{8} \]

总面积是 \(\frac{1}{2}\)，所以失败概率：The total area is \(\frac{1}{2}\), so the failure probability is:

\[ \frac{\frac{3}{8}}{\frac{1}{2}}=\frac{3}{4} \]

成功概率：Success probability:

\[ 1-\frac{3}{4}=\frac{1}{4}=25\% \]

七、最终答案7. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{25\%} \]
最终答案：B，25%。Final answer: B, 25%.
