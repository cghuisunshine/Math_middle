一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：若 √(m²+59) 是整数，求正整数 m。 Original image: If √(m²+59) is an integer, find the positive integer m.
若 √(m² + 59) 是整数， 求正整数 m。

二、核心想法：把根号设成整数2. Main Idea: Set the Radical Equal to an Integer

因为 √(m²+59) 是整数，设它等于 n。Since √(m²+59) is an integer, let it be n.

√(m² + 59) = n

两边平方：Square both sides:

m² + 59 = n²

移项得到平方差：Rearrange to get a difference of squares:

n² - m² = 59

三、平方差分解3. Factor the Difference of Squares

利用公式：Use the formula:

n² - m² = (n-m)(n+m)

所以：So:

(n - m)(n + m) = 59

因为 59 是质数，且 n、m 都是正整数，所以只能：Since 59 is prime and n,m are positive integers, the only possibility is:

n - m = 1 n + m = 59

四、解方程4. Solve

两式相加：Add the two equations:

(n-m) + (n+m) = 1 + 59 2n = 60 n = 30

代回 n+m=59：Substitute into n+m=59:

30 + m = 59 m = 29

检验：Check:

√(29² + 59) = √(841 + 59) = √900 = 30
答案：m = 29Answer: m = 29

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

m = 29
