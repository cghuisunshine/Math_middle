一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：\(S_{\triangle ABC}=12\)，图中 \(BC=6\)，求 \(\frac{AB}{AC}\) 的最小值。Original image: \(S_{\triangle ABC}=12\), and \(BC=6\). Find the minimum of \(\frac{AB}{AC}\).
\[ S_{\triangle ABC}=12,\qquad BC=6,\qquad \text{求 }\frac{AB}{AC}\text{ 的最小值。} \]

二、先求高2. First Find the Height

设从 \(A\) 向 \(BC\) 所在直线作垂线，垂足为 \(D\)。因为底边 \(BC=6\)，面积为 12：Drop a perpendicular from \(A\) to the line \(BC\), with foot \(D\). Since the base \(BC=6\) and the area is 12:

\[ \frac12\cdot BC\cdot AD=12 \]
\[ \frac12\cdot6\cdot AD=12 \]
\[ AD=4 \]
所以点 \(A\) 一定在距离直线 \(BC\) 为 4 的平行线上。So point \(A\) lies on a line parallel to \(BC\), at distance 4 from it.

三、设坐标3. Set Coordinates

取：Let:

\[ B=(0,0),\qquad C=(6,0) \]

因为高为 4，可以设：Since the height is 4, set:

\[ A=(x,4) \]

四、用勾股定理表示两边4. Express the Two Sides

\[ AB^2=x^2+4^2=x^2+16 \]
\[ AC^2=(x-6)^2+4^2=(x-6)^2+16 \]

要证明 \(\frac{AB}{AC}\) 最小为 \(\frac12\)，只要证明：To prove that the minimum of \(\frac{AB}{AC}\) is \(\frac12\), it is enough to prove:

\[ 4AB^2\ge AC^2 \]

五、关键配方5. Key Completing Square Step

\[ 4AB^2-AC^2 =4(x^2+16)-\left((x-6)^2+16\right) \]
\[ =4x^2+64-(x^2-12x+36+16) \]
\[ =3x^2+12x+12 \]
\[ =3(x+2)^2 \]

因为平方一定大于等于 0，所以：Since a square is always nonnegative:

\[ 4AB^2-AC^2=3(x+2)^2\ge0 \]
\[ \frac{AB}{AC}\ge\frac12 \]

六、等号成立6. Equality Case

当 \(x=-2\) 时取等号。此时：Equality holds when \(x=-2\). Then:

\[ A=(-2,4) \]
\[ AB=\sqrt{(-2)^2+4^2}=2\sqrt5 \]
\[ AC=\sqrt{(-8)^2+4^2}=4\sqrt5 \]
\[ \frac{AB}{AC}=\frac{2\sqrt5}{4\sqrt5}=\frac12 \]
最小值：\(\frac12\)Minimum value: \(\frac12\)

七、方法二：相似三角形 + 圆中最远点7. Method 2: Similar Triangles + Farthest Point on a Circle

补充方法：按图作辅助点 \(D,P,O\)。核心思想是先把比值化成 \(\frac{4}{DP}\)，再把问题转化为求 \(DP\) 的最大值。Supplementary method: construct the auxiliary points \(D,P,O\) as shown. The key idea is to turn the ratio into \(\frac{4}{DP}\), and then maximize \(DP\).

由面积条件 \(S_{\triangle ABC}=12\)、底边 \(BC=6\)，可知高为 4。所以在点 \(C\) 的正上方作点 \(D\)，使：From \(S_{\triangle ABC}=12\) and \(BC=6\), the height is 4. So place point \(D\) directly above \(C\) such that:

\[ DC=4 \]

按图中的辅助构造，可得到一组相似三角形，从而把原比值转化为：Using the auxiliary construction in the diagram, we obtain a pair of similar triangles, so the original ratio becomes:

\[ \frac{AB}{AC}=\frac{DC}{DP}=\frac{4}{DP} \]

因此，要求 \(\frac{AB}{AC}\) 的最小值，只需要让 \(DP\) 最大。Therefore, to minimize \(\frac{AB}{AC}\), it is enough to maximize \(DP\).

再看图形，点 \(P\) 在以 \(BC\) 为直径的圆上。设 \(O\) 是 \(BC\) 的中点，则：Now observe that point \(P\) lies on the circle with diameter \(BC\). Let \(O\) be the midpoint of \(BC\). Then:

\[ OB=OC=\frac{BC}{2}=3 \]

因为 \(DC=4\)，且 \(OC=3\)，所以：Since \(DC=4\) and \(OC=3\), we have:

\[ OD=\sqrt{OC^2+CD^2}=\sqrt{3^2+4^2}=5 \]

圆上点 \(P\) 到定点 \(D\) 的距离最大时，应当在 \(D,O,P\) 三点共线，且 \(P\) 取圆上离 \(D\) 最远的点。因此：The distance from the fixed point \(D\) to a point \(P\) on the circle is greatest when \(D,O,P\) are collinear and \(P\) is the farthest point on the circle from \(D\). Hence:

\[ DP_{\max}=DO+OP=5+3=8 \]

于是：So:

\[ \frac{AB}{AC}=\frac{4}{DP}\ge \frac{4}{8}=\frac12 \]

当 \(DP=8\) 时取得等号，所以最小值就是：Equality holds when \(DP=8\), so the minimum value is:

\[ \boxed{\frac12} \]
方法二结论：\(\frac{AB}{AC}\) 的最小值仍然是 \(\frac12\)。Method 2 conclusion: the minimum of \(\frac{AB}{AC}\) is again \(\frac12\).
