一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：一根绳子绕树，绕 3 圈剩 1 米，绕 5 圈少 7 米，绕 1 圈还剩几米？A rope is wrapped around a tree. After 3 loops, 1 meter remains; after 5 loops, it is 7 meters short. How many meters remain after 1 loop?
一根绳子绕树： 绕 3 圈剩 1 米； 绕 5 圈少 7 米； 问：绕 1 圈还剩几米？

二、解法一：差量法2. Solution 1: Difference Method

从绕 3 圈到绕 5 圈，多绕了 2 圈。From 3 loops to 5 loops, there are 2 more loops.

2 圈 = 1 + 7 = 8 米 1 圈 = 8 ÷ 2 = 4 米

绕 3 圈剩 1 米，若只绕 1 圈，就少绕 2 圈，所以多剩 8 米：After 3 loops, 1 meter remains. If only 1 loop is made, that is 2 fewer loops, so 8 more meters remain:

绕 1 圈剩下 = 1 + 2×4 = 9 米
答案：9 米Answer: 9 meters

四、解法二：设未知数4. Solution 2: Set Variables

设树的一圈长度为 x 米，绳子总长为 L 米。Let one loop around the tree be x meters and the rope length be L meters.

L = 3x + 1 L = 5x - 7
3x + 1 = 5x - 7 8 = 2x x = 4
L = 3×4 + 1 = 13 绕 1 圈还剩 = 13 - 4 = 9 米
答案：9 米Answer: 9 meters

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

绕 1 圈还剩 9 米
最终答案：9 米Final answer: 9 meters
