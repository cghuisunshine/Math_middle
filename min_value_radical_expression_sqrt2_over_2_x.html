一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：求 \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}x+\sqrt{x^2-6x+10}\)，其中 \(0<x<3\)，的最小值。 Original image: Find the minimum of \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}x+\sqrt{x^2-6x+10}\), where \(0<x<3\).
\[ \text{求 }\frac{\sqrt2}{2}x+\sqrt{x^2-6x+10}\quad(0<x<3)\text{ 的最小值} \]

二、先变形2. First Rewrite

根号里面先配方：Complete the square inside the radical:

\[ x^2-6x+10=(x-3)^2+1 \]

令：Let:

\[ t=3-x \]

因为 \(0<x<3\)，所以 \(0<t<3\)。原式变为：Since \(0<x<3\), we have \(0<t<3\). The expression becomes:

\[ \frac{\sqrt2}{2}x+\sqrt{(x-3)^2+1} = \frac{3-t}{\sqrt2}+\sqrt{t^2+1} \]

三、解法一：用平方差放缩3. Solution 1: Use an Inequality

关键是证明：The key is to prove:

\[ \sqrt{t^2+1}\ge \frac{t+1}{\sqrt2} \]

为什么成立？两边都是正数，可以平方比较：Why is this true? Both sides are positive, so square both sides:

\[ t^2+1\ge \frac{(t+1)^2}{2} \]

整理后得到：After simplifying, we get:

\[ 2t^2+2\ge t^2+2t+1 \]
\[ t^2-2t+1\ge0 \]
\[ (t-1)^2\ge0 \]

所以不等式成立，且当 \(t=1\) 时取等号。So the inequality holds, with equality when \(t=1\).

代回原式：Substitute into the expression:

\[ \frac{3-t}{\sqrt2}+\sqrt{t^2+1} \ge \frac{3-t}{\sqrt2}+\frac{t+1}{\sqrt2} = \frac4{\sqrt2} = 2\sqrt2 \]

当 \(t=1\)，即 \(x=2\) 时取等号。Equality occurs when \(t=1\), i.e. \(x=2\).

最小值：\(2\sqrt2\)Minimum value: \(2\sqrt2\)

四、解法二：几何折线法4. Solution 2: Geometric Broken-Line Method

把两项分别看成两个三角形中的边：Interpret the two terms as sides in two triangles:

  • 第一项 \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}x=\frac{x}{\sqrt2}\) 是一个斜边为 \(x\) 的等腰直角三角形的直角边；The first term \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}x=\frac{x}{\sqrt2}\) is a leg of an isosceles right triangle whose hypotenuse is \(x\).
  • 第二项 \(\sqrt{x^2-6x+10}=\sqrt{(3-x)^2+1}\) 是另一个直角三角形的斜边，另外两边分别是 \(3-x\) 和 \(1\)。The second term \(\sqrt{x^2-6x+10}=\sqrt{(3-x)^2+1}\) is the hypotenuse of another right triangle with legs \(3-x\) and \(1\).
示意图：把原式看成折线 \(MP+PQ\) 的长度。蓝色边表示第一项，橙黄色完整直角三角形 \(PQR\) 表示第二项，灰色虚线折线表示点 \(P\) 取其他位置时的情况，红色虚线表示当折线最小时，折线会拉直成一条线段，即 \(M,P,Q\) 共线。这里画的是取等时的情形 \(x=2\)。 Illustration: view the expression as the broken-line length \(MP+PQ\). The blue edges represent the first term, the full yellow-orange right triangle \(PQR\) represents the second term, the gray dashed broken line shows a different position of \(P\), and the red dashed segment shows that when the broken line is minimal, it straightens into one segment, so \(M,P,Q\) are collinear. This picture shows the equality case \(x=2\).

设 \(O=(0,0)\)，\(P=(x,0)\)，\(Q=(3,1)\)。在 \(OP\) 的下方作等腰直角三角形 \(OMP\)，使 \(OP\) 是斜边，那么：Let \(O=(0,0)\), \(P=(x,0)\), and \(Q=(3,1)\). Construct an isosceles right triangle \(OMP\) below \(OP\) with \(OP\) as the hypotenuse. Then:

\[ MP=\frac{OP}{\sqrt2}=\frac{x}{\sqrt2} \]

而：Also:

\[ PQ=\sqrt{(3-x)^2+1}=\sqrt{x^2-6x+10} \]

所以原式正好就是：So the original expression is exactly:

\[ S=MP+PQ \]

由坐标可知 \(M=(\frac{x}{2},-\frac{x}{2})\)，所以点 \(M\) 总在固定直线 \(y=-x\) 上。于是，我们的问题变成：在这条固定直线上取点 \(M\)，使相邻两段折线 \(MP\) 与 \(PQ\) 的和最小。From the coordinates, \(M=(\frac{x}{2},-\frac{x}{2})\), so \(M\) always lies on the fixed line \(y=-x\). Thus the problem becomes: choose a point \(M\) on this fixed line so that the sum of the two adjacent segments \(MP\) and \(PQ\) is as small as possible.

\[ \text{求折线 }MPQ\text{ 的最小长度} \]

根据三角形两边之和大于第三边：By the triangle inequality:

\[ MP+PQ\ge MQ \]

因此，只要让 \(MQ\) 最短即可。由于 \(M\) 在直线 \(y=-x\) 上移动，所以 \(MQ\) 的最小值就是点 \(Q(3,1)\) 到直线 \(y=-x\) 的距离：Therefore, we only need to minimize \(MQ\). Since \(M\) moves on the line \(y=-x\), the minimum of \(MQ\) is the distance from \(Q(3,1)\) to the line \(y=-x\):

\[ \operatorname{dist}\bigl((3,1),\,x+y=0\bigr)=\frac{|3+1|}{\sqrt{1^2+1^2}}=\frac4{\sqrt2}=2\sqrt2 \]

所以：Hence:

\[ S\ge 2\sqrt2 \]

当 \(M\) 是垂足时取等号。此时垂足是 \((1,-1)\)，对应 \(P=(2,0)\)，所以 \(x=2\)。Equality holds when \(M\) is the foot of the perpendicular. That foot is \((1,-1)\), corresponding to \(P=(2,0)\), so \(x=2\).

最小值：\(2\sqrt2\)Minimum value: \(2\sqrt2\)

五、解法三：导数验证5. Solution 3: Check with Derivatives

原函数为：The function is:

\[ f(x)=\frac{x}{\sqrt2}+\sqrt{(x-3)^2+1} \]

求导：Differentiate:

\[ f'(x)=\frac1{\sqrt2}+\frac{x-3}{\sqrt{(x-3)^2+1}} \]

令 \(f'(x)=0\)：Set \(f'(x)=0\):

\[ \frac{3-x}{\sqrt{(3-x)^2+1}}=\frac1{\sqrt2} \]

两边平方：Square both sides:

\[ \frac{(3-x)^2}{(3-x)^2+1}=\frac12 \]
\[ (3-x)^2=1 \]

因为 \(0<x<3\)，所以 \(3-x>0\)，得到：Since \(0<x<3\), \(3-x>0\), so:

\[ 3-x=1\quad\Rightarrow\quad x=2 \]

代入：Substitute:

\[ f(2)=\frac{2}{\sqrt2}+\sqrt{4-12+10}=\sqrt2+\sqrt2=2\sqrt2 \]
最小值：\(2\sqrt2\)Minimum value: \(2\sqrt2\)

六、最终答案6. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{2\sqrt2} \]
最终答案：\(2\sqrt2\)Final answer: \(2\sqrt2\)
