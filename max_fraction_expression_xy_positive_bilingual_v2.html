一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[ x>0,\quad y>0 \]
\[ \text{求 }\frac{x}{x^2+1}+\frac{2y}{x^2+y^2+1}\text{ 的最大值} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac{3\sqrt3}{4}} \]
最大值为 \(\frac{3\sqrt3}{4}\)，选 B。The maximum is \(\frac{3\sqrt3}{4}\), so the answer is B.

三、固定 \(x\)，先求关于 \(y\) 的最大值3. Fix \(x\), First Maximize with Respect to \(y\)

\[ E=\frac{x}{x^2+1}+\frac{2y}{x^2+y^2+1} \]

当 \(x\) 固定时，令 \(A=x^2+1\)，第二项为：When \(x\) is fixed, let \(A=x^2+1\). The second term is:

\[ \frac{2y}{y^2+A} \]

由基本不等式：By AM-GM:

\[ y^2+A\ge 2y\sqrt A \]
\[ \frac{2y}{y^2+A}\le \frac{1}{\sqrt A}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}} \]

等号在 \(y^2=A=x^2+1\) 时成立。Equality holds when \(y^2=A=x^2+1\).

四、化为一个变量4. Reduce to One Variable

\[ E\le \frac{x}{x^2+1}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}} \]

令 \(x=\tan\theta\)，其中 \(0<\theta<\frac{\pi}{2}\)。Let \(x=\tan\theta\), where \(0<\theta<\frac{\pi}{2}\).

\[ \frac{x}{x^2+1}=\sin\theta\cos\theta,\qquad \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}}=\cos\theta \]
\[ E\le \cos\theta(1+\sin\theta) \]

五、求最大值5. Find the Maximum

设 \(t=\sin\theta\)，则Let \(t=\sin\theta\). Then

\[ 0
\[ F(t)=(1+t)\sqrt{1-t^2} \]

平方后求最大点：Square it to find the maximizing point:

\[ F(t)^2=(1+t)^2(1-t^2)=(1+t)^3(1-t) \]

令 \(G(t)=(1+t)^3(1-t)\)，则Let \(G(t)=(1+t)^3(1-t)\). Then

\[ G'(t)=3(1+t)^2(1-t)-(1+t)^3 \]
\[ G'(t)=2(1+t)^2(1-2t) \]
\[ G'(t)=0\Longrightarrow t=\frac12 \]
\[ \sin\theta=\frac12,\quad \cos\theta=\frac{\sqrt3}{2} \]
\[ E_{\max}=\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\left(1+\frac12\right)=\frac{3\sqrt3}{4} \]

六、另一种解法：换元后用均值不等式6. Another Method: Substitute and Use AM-GM

由前面已经得到的不等式：From the earlier estimate:

\[ E\le \frac{x}{x^2+1}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}} \]

令 \(x=\tan\theta\)，其中 \(0<\theta<\frac{\pi}{2}\)。则Let \(x=\tan\theta\), where \(0<\theta<\frac{\pi}{2}\). Then

\[ \frac{x}{x^2+1}=\sin\theta\cos\theta, \qquad \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}}=\cos\theta \]
\[ E\le \sin\theta\cos\theta+\cos\theta=(1+\sin\theta)\cos\theta \]

把它写成根式形式：Rewrite it in square-root form:

\[ (1+\sin\theta)\cos\theta =\sqrt{(1+\sin\theta)^2\cos^2\theta} =\sqrt{(1+\sin\theta)^3(1-\sin\theta)} \]
\[ =\sqrt{\frac{(1+\sin\theta)^3(3-3\sin\theta)}{3}} \]

对四个非负数 \(1+\sin\theta,\ 1+\sin\theta,\ 1+\sin\theta,\ 3-3\sin\theta\) 使用均值不等式：Apply AM-GM to the four nonnegative numbers \(1+\sin\theta,\ 1+\sin\theta,\ 1+\sin\theta,\ 3-3\sin\theta\):

\[ \frac{(1+\sin\theta)+(1+\sin\theta)+(1+\sin\theta)+(3-3\sin\theta)}{4} \ge \sqrt[4]{(1+\sin\theta)^3(3-3\sin\theta)} \]

因为上式左边的分子恒等于 \(6\)，所以Since the numerator on the left is always \(6\), we get

\[ (1+\sin\theta)^3(3-3\sin\theta)\le \left(\frac{6}{4}\right)^4=\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^4 \]
\[ E\le \sqrt{\frac{(3/2)^4}{3}}=\frac{3\sqrt3}{4} \]

等号成立时，四个数相等，即Equality holds when the four numbers are equal, that is,

\[ 1+\sin\theta=3-3\sin\theta \]
\[ 4\sin\theta=2 \Longrightarrow \sin\theta=\frac12 \]
\[ \theta=30^\circ, \qquad x=\tan\theta=\frac1{\sqrt3} \]

再结合前面第二项取等时需要 \(y^2=x^2+1\)，且 \(y>0\)，于是Also, equality in the earlier bound for the second term requires \(y^2=x^2+1\), and since \(y>0\),

\[ y=\sqrt{x^2+1}=\sqrt{\frac13+1}=\frac2{\sqrt3} \]
因此，这个方法同样得到最大值 \(\frac{3\sqrt3}{4}\)。 Therefore, this method also gives the maximum value \(\frac{3\sqrt3}{4}\).

七、等号成立条件7. Equality Conditions

\[ x=\tan30^\circ=\frac1{\sqrt3} \]
\[ y=\sqrt{x^2+1}=\sqrt{\frac13+1}=\frac2{\sqrt3} \]
当 \(x=\frac1{\sqrt3},\ y=\frac2{\sqrt3}\) 时，最大值可以取到。 The maximum is achieved when \(x=\frac1{\sqrt3},\ y=\frac2{\sqrt3}\).
