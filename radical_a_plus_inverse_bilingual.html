一、题目1. Problem

题目原图 Original problem image
\[ a=\sqrt{2+\sqrt3}+\sqrt{2-\sqrt3} \]
\[ \text{求 }a+\frac1a \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac{7\sqrt6}{6}} \]
最终结果是 \(\frac{7\sqrt6}{6}\)。 The final result is \(\frac{7\sqrt6}{6}\).

三、核心想法：先求 \(a^2\)3. Key Idea: First Find \(a^2\)

因为 \(a\) 是两个根式的和，直接求 \(a+\frac1a\) 不方便。先把 \(a\) 平方，根号会明显化简。 Because \(a\) is a sum of two radicals, directly finding \(a+\frac1a\) is not convenient. Squaring \(a\) makes the radicals simplify nicely.

\[ a=\sqrt{2+\sqrt3}+\sqrt{2-\sqrt3} \]
\[ a^2= \left(\sqrt{2+\sqrt3}+\sqrt{2-\sqrt3}\right)^2 \]

四、展开并化简4. Expand and Simplify

利用公式： Use the identity:

\[ (m+n)^2=m^2+n^2+2mn \]

所以： So:

\[ a^2=(2+\sqrt3)+(2-\sqrt3) +2\sqrt{(2+\sqrt3)(2-\sqrt3)} \]

先看中间的乘积： Now simplify the product inside the radical:

\[ (2+\sqrt3)(2-\sqrt3)=2^2-(\sqrt3)^2=4-3=1 \]

因此： Therefore:

\[ a^2=4+2\sqrt1=4+2=6 \]

五、由 \(a^2=6\) 得到 \(a\)5. Get \(a\) from \(a^2=6\)

因为两个根号都是正数，所以 \(a>0\)。 Since both radicals are positive, \(a>0\).

\[ a^2=6 \]
\[ a=\sqrt6 \]
这里不能写成 \(a=\pm\sqrt6\)，因为原式中 \(a\) 明显是正数。 Do not write \(a=\pm\sqrt6\), because the original expression for \(a\) is clearly positive.

六、代入目标式6. Substitute into the Target Expression

\[ a+\frac1a=\sqrt6+\frac1{\sqrt6} \]

通分： Use a common denominator:

\[ \sqrt6+\frac1{\sqrt6} = \frac6{\sqrt6}+\frac1{\sqrt6} = \frac7{\sqrt6} \]

有理化分母： Rationalize the denominator:

\[ \frac7{\sqrt6}\cdot\frac{\sqrt6}{\sqrt6} = \frac{7\sqrt6}{6} \]

七、最终答案7. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac{7\sqrt6}{6}} \]
所以 \(a+\frac1a=\frac{7\sqrt6}{6}\)。 Therefore, \(a+\frac1a=\frac{7\sqrt6}{6}\).
