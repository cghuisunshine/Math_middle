一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[a+b=1\]
\[\text{求 }\sqrt{2a+1}+\sqrt{2b+1}\text{ 的最大值}\]

二、答案2. Answer

\[\boxed{2\sqrt2}\]
当 \(a=b=\frac12\) 时取得最大值。The maximum is attained when \(a=b=\frac12\).

三、方法一：柯西不等式3. Method 1: Cauchy's Inequality

令：

Let:

\[x=2a+1,\qquad y=2b+1\]

由 \(a+b=1\)：

Since \(a+b=1\):

\[x+y=4\]

由柯西不等式：

By Cauchy's inequality:

\[(\sqrt{x}+\sqrt{y})^2\le(1^2+1^2)(x+y)=8\]
\[\sqrt{x}+\sqrt{y}\le2\sqrt2\]
所以最大值不超过 \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\)。Therefore, the expression is at most \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\).

四、方法二：均值不等式4. Method 2: AM-GM / Concavity

仍令 \(x=2a+1,\ y=2b+1\)，则 \(x+y=4\)。

Again let \(x=2a+1,\ y=2b+1\), so \(x+y=4\).

\[\sqrt{x}+\sqrt{y}\le2\sqrt{\frac{x+y}{2}}=2\sqrt2\]
等号在 \(x=y=2\) 时成立，即 \(a=b=\frac12\)。Equality holds when \(x=y=2\), that is, when \(a=b=\frac12\).

五、方法三：几何构造5. Method 3: Geometric Construction

几何构造示意图Geometric construction diagram

令：

Let:

\[p=\sqrt{2a+1},\qquad q=\sqrt{2b+1}\]

因为 \(a+b=1\)，所以：

Since \(a+b=1\):

\[p^2+q^2=(2a+1)+(2b+1)=4\]

图中左右两个直角三角形的斜边长度都为：

In the diagram, the hypotenuse of each right triangle has length:

\[\sqrt{p^2+q^2}=2\]

两条长度为 \(2\) 的斜边互相垂直，因此两个外端点之间的距离为：

The two length-\(2\) segments are perpendicular, so the distance between their outer endpoints is:

\[\sqrt{2^2+2^2}=2\sqrt2\]

这条线段的水平投影为：

The horizontal projection of this segment is:

\[p+q=\sqrt{2a+1}+\sqrt{2b+1}\]

水平投影不超过线段长度，因此：

A horizontal projection cannot exceed the segment's length, so:

\[p+q\le2\sqrt2\]

当顶端线段水平时取等号，即 \(p=q\)。

Equality occurs when the top segment is horizontal, so \(p=q\).

\[p=q\Longrightarrow a=b\Longrightarrow a=b=\frac12\]
所以几何方法同样得到最大值 \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\)。Thus the geometric method also gives the maximum \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\).

六、最终答案6. Final Answer

\[\boxed{\max\left(\sqrt{2a+1}+\sqrt{2b+1}\right)=2\sqrt2}\]
当 \(a=b=\frac12\) 时取得最大值。The maximum is attained when \(a=b=\frac12\).
