\[ 6^x=x^{18} \]

三、方法一：看成 \(6\) 的幂3. Method 1: Treat \(x\) as a Power of \(6\)

因为左边是 \(6^x\)，右边是 \(x^{18}\)。如果 \(x\) 本身也是 \(6\) 的幂，就很容易比较指数。Since the left side is \(6^x\) and the right side is \(x^{18}\), it is natural to try \(x\) as a power of \(6\).

设：Let:

\[ x=6^a \]

则：Then:

\[ x^{18}=(6^a)^{18}=6^{18a} \]

原方程变成：The original equation becomes:

\[ 6^x=6^{18a} \]

底数相同，所以指数相等：The bases are the same, so the exponents are equal:

\[ x=18a \]

又因为 \(x=6^a\)，所以：Since \(x=6^a\), we get:

\[ 6^a=18a \]

试整数 \(a\)：Try integer \(a\):

\[ a=2,\qquad 6^2=36,\qquad 18\times2=36 \]

所以：Therefore:

\[ x=6^2=36 \]

四、方法二：开 \(18\) 次方4. Method 2: Take the \(18\)-th Root

如果求正数解，可以对两边开 \(18\) 次方：For positive solutions, take the \(18\)-th root on both sides:

\[ (6^x)^{1/18}=(x^{18})^{1/18} \]

得到：This gives:

\[ 6^{x/18}=x \]

把 \(x=36\) 代入验证：Check \(x=36\):

\[ 6^{36/18}=6^2=36 \]

所以 \(x=36\) 成立。Thus \(x=36\) works.

五、直接验证5. Direct Check

\[ 6^{36}=6^{36} \]
\[ 36^{18}=(6^2)^{18}=6^{36} \]

左右相等，所以：Both sides are equal, so:

\[ \boxed{x=36} \]
