一、题目公式1. Formula

图片中的公式是二维柯西不等式。The formula in the image is the two-dimensional Cauchy inequality.
\[\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\ge ax+by\]
\[|ax+by|\le \sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\]

二、平方化证明2. Proof by Squaring

先证明：First prove:

\[(ax+by)^2\le (a^2+b^2)(x^2+y^2)\]

把右边减左边：Subtract the left side from the right side:

\[(a^2+b^2)(x^2+y^2)-(ax+by)^2\]

展开：Expand:

\[=a^2x^2+a^2y^2+b^2x^2+b^2y^2-(a^2x^2+2abxy+b^2y^2)\]

消去相同项：Cancel the same terms:

\[=a^2y^2+b^2x^2-2abxy\]

配成完全平方：Make a perfect square:

\[= (ay-bx)^2\ge 0\]

所以：Therefore:

\[(a^2+b^2)(x^2+y^2)\ge (ax+by)^2\]

两边开平方：Taking square roots gives:

\[\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\ge |ax+by|\]

因此也有：Therefore, we also have:

\[\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\ge ax+by\]

三、等号成立条件3. Equality Condition

等号成立时：Equality holds when:

\[(ay-bx)^2=0\]
\[ay=bx\]

也就是两个向量 \((a,b)\) 和 \((x,y)\) 同方向或反方向。若求 \(ax+by\) 的最大值，取同方向：This means \((a,b)\) and \((x,y)\) are in the same or opposite direction. For maximizing \(ax+by\), take the same direction:

\[(x,y)=k(a,b),\quad k\gt0\]
记忆：两个向量方向相同，点积最大。Memory tip: the dot product is largest when the vectors point in the same direction.

四、几何理解4. Geometric Meaning

\[u\cdot v=ax+by\]
\[|u|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2},\qquad |v|=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\]
\[u\cdot v=|u||v|\cos\theta\le |u||v|\]

五、几何证明（二）：直角梯形拼图法5. Geometric Proof II: Right-Trapezoid Construction

再加入一种与你提供图片一致的几何证明。把图中两段白色斜边看成一个直角三角形的两条直角边，而上方紫色标出的整段看成这个大直角三角形的斜边。Here is another geometric proof matching the image you provided. Treat the two white slanted segments as the two perpendicular legs of a right triangle, and the full purple segment on the top as the hypotenuse of that larger right triangle.

几何拼图法：左边看作斜边，右边看作一条直角边。Geometric construction: the left-hand side is viewed as a hypotenuse, while the right-hand side is one leg.

记Let

\[ L=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2},\qquad R=ax+by \]

由图中的相似三角形拼接关系，可得到这个大直角三角形的三边分别是：From the similar-triangle construction in the diagram, the three sides of the larger right triangle are:

\[ \text{一条直角边}=ax+by \]
\[ \text{另一条直角边}=|ay-bx| \]
\[ \text{斜边}=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2} \]

因此根据勾股定理：Therefore, by the Pythagorean theorem:

\[ L^2=(ax+by)^2+(ay-bx)^2 \]

展开右边：Expand the right-hand side:

\[ \begin{aligned} (ax+by)^2+(ay-bx)^2 &=a^2x^2+2abxy+b^2y^2+a^2y^2-2abxy+b^2x^2\\ &=a^2x^2+a^2y^2+b^2x^2+b^2y^2\\ &=(a^2+b^2)(x^2+y^2) \end{aligned} \]

所以：So:

\[ L^2=(a^2+b^2)(x^2+y^2) \]
\[ L=\sqrt{(ax+by)^2+(ay-bx)^2} \]

而在直角三角形中，斜边一定不小于任何一条直角边，所以：In a right triangle, the hypotenuse is always at least as large as either leg, so:

\[ L\ge R \]
\[ \boxed{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\ge ax+by} \]
这个几何法的核心想法是：把左边构造成“斜边”，把右边构造成“直角边”，于是“斜边 ≥ 直角边”立刻得到柯西不等式。The key geometric idea is: construct the left-hand side as a hypotenuse and the right-hand side as a leg. Then “hypotenuse ≥ leg” immediately gives Cauchy’s inequality.

六、例子6. Example

\[x^2+y^2=4,\qquad \text{求 }4x+3y\text{ 的最大值}\]
\[4x+3y\le \sqrt{4^2+3^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}=5\cdot2=10\]

当 \((x,y)\) 与 \((4,3)\) 同方向时取等号：Equality holds when \((x,y)\) has the same direction as \((4,3)\):

\[(x,y)=\frac{2}{5}(4,3)=\left(\frac{8}{5},\frac{6}{5}\right)\]
最大值：10Maximum value: 10

七、最终结论7. Final Conclusion

\[\boxed{|ax+by|\le \sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\]
\[\boxed{ax+by\le \sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\]
这就是二维柯西不等式。This is the two-dimensional Cauchy inequality.
