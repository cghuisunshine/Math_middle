一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：等边三角形 \(ABC\)，\(P\) 是内部动点，求 \(PA+PB+PC\) 的最小值。Original image: In equilateral triangle \(ABC\), point \(P\) moves inside. Find the minimum of \(PA+PB+PC\).
\[ \text{设等边三角形边长为 }a,\quad \text{求 }PA+PB+PC\text{ 的最小值。} \]
如果题目给的是具体边长，把下面答案中的 \(a\) 换成那个边长即可。If the side length is given, replace \(a\) below with that side length.

二、结论2. Result

\[ \boxed{PA+PB+PC\ge \sqrt3a} \]
\[ \text{最小值在 }P\text{ 为等边三角形中心时取得。} \]
最小值：\(\sqrt3a\)Minimum value: \(\sqrt3a\)

三、解法一：中心点直接算3. Solution 1: Calculate at the Center

等边三角形的中心到三个顶点距离相等。若边长为 \(a\)，高为：The center of an equilateral triangle is equally distant from the three vertices. If the side length is \(a\), the height is:

\[ h=\frac{\sqrt3}{2}a \]

中心到顶点的距离是高的 \(\frac23\)：The distance from the center to a vertex is \(\frac23\) of the height:

\[ AO=BO=CO=\frac23h=\frac23\cdot\frac{\sqrt3}{2}a=\frac{a}{\sqrt3} \]

所以当 \(P=O\) 时：So when \(P=O\):

\[ PA+PB+PC=3\cdot\frac{a}{\sqrt3}=\sqrt3a \]

四、解法二：旋转证明为什么这是最小4. Solution 2: Rotation Proof for the Minimum

在 \(BC\) 的外侧作等边三角形 \(BCD\)。把 \(\triangle BPC\) 绕点 \(B\) 旋转 \(60^\circ\)，使 \(C\) 转到 \(D\)，\(P\) 转到 \(P'\)。Construct equilateral triangle \(BCD\) outside side \(BC\). Rotate \(\triangle BPC\) about \(B\) by \(60^\circ\), so \(C\) moves to \(D\), and \(P\) moves to \(P'\).

旋转保持长度不变，并且 \(\triangle BPP'\) 是等边三角形，因此：Rotation preserves lengths, and \(\triangle BPP'\) is equilateral, so:

\[ PB=PP',\qquad PC=P'D \]

于是：Thus:

\[ PA+PB+PC=PA+PP'+P'D \]

右边就是从 \(A\) 到 \(D\) 的一条折线路径，所以它一定不小于直线段 \(AD\)：The right side is a broken path from \(A\) to \(D\), so it is at least the straight segment \(AD\):

\[ PA+PP'+P'D\ge AD \]

而 \(AD\) 是由两个等边三角形的高组成：Here, \(AD\) is made of the heights of two equilateral triangles:

\[ AD=2\cdot\frac{\sqrt3}{2}a=\sqrt3a \]

所以：Therefore:

\[ PA+PB+PC\ge \sqrt3a \]

当折线 \(A-P-P'-D\) 拉直成一条直线时取等号，此时 \(P\) 正好是等边三角形的中心。Equality holds when the broken path \(A-P-P'-D\) becomes a straight line. Then \(P\) is exactly the center of the equilateral triangle.

最小值：\(\sqrt3a\)Minimum value: \(\sqrt3a\)

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{\sqrt3a} \]
若边长为 \(a\)，最小值是 \(\sqrt3a\)。If the side length is \(a\), the minimum value is \(\sqrt3a\).
