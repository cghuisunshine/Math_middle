一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：解方程 \(33^x-32^x=65\)。Original image: Solve \(33^x-32^x=65\).
\[ 33^x-32^x=65 \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{x=2} \]
方程的解是 \(x=2\)。The solution is \(x=2\).

三、核心观察：65 可以写成 33+323. Key Observation: 65 Can Be Written as 33+32

右边：The right-hand side is:

\[ 65=33+32 \]

这让我们想到平方差公式：This reminds us of the difference of squares formula:

\[ a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b) \]

四、代入验证4. Substitute and Check

当 \(x=2\) 时：When \(x=2\):

\[ 33^x-32^x=33^2-32^2 \]
\[ =(33-32)(33+32) \]
\[ =1\times65=65 \]
所以 \(x=2\)。So \(x=2\).

五、为什么自然想到 \(x=2\)5. Why \(x=2\) Is Natural

因为：Because:

\[ 33-32=1 \]
\[ 33^2-32^2=(33-32)(33+32) \]
\[ =33+32=65 \]
所以看到 \(65\) 和相邻整数 \(33,32\)，最自然就是尝试平方差，也就是 \(x=2\)。So when we see \(65\) together with the consecutive numbers \(33\) and \(32\), the most natural idea is the difference of squares, meaning \(x=2\).

六、最终答案6. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{x=2} \]
