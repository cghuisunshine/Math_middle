一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[ x>0,\quad y>0,\quad x^2+y^2=5 \]
\[ \text{求 }\frac{2}{x+2}+\frac{1}{y+3}\text{ 的最小值} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac34} \]
最小值为 \(\frac34\)，当 \(x=2,\ y=1\) 时取得。The minimum is \(\frac34\), achieved when \(x=2,\ y=1\).

三、先找等号点3. First Find the Equality Point

由 \(x^2+y^2=5\)，一个很自然的整数点是： From \(x^2+y^2=5\), a natural integer point is:

\[ x=2,\qquad y=1 \]

代入目标式： Substitute into the target expression:

\[ \frac{2}{2+2}+\frac{1}{1+3} =\frac24+\frac14 =\frac34 \]
所以猜测最小值是 \(\frac34\)。接下来证明任何情况下都不小于 \(\frac34\)。 So we guess the minimum is \(\frac34\). Now we prove the expression is always at least \(\frac34\).

四、构造两个下界4. Build Two Lower Bounds

围绕等号点 \(x=2,\ y=1\)，构造两个恒成立的不等式： Around the equality point \(x=2,\ y=1\), build two inequalities that are always true:

\[ \frac{2}{x+2}\ge \frac{6-x}{8} \]

因为：because:

\[ \frac{2}{x+2}-\frac{6-x}{8} = \frac{(x-2)^2}{8(x+2)} \ge0 \]
\[ \frac{1}{y+3}\ge \frac{5-y}{16} \]

因为：because:

\[ \frac{1}{y+3}-\frac{5-y}{16} = \frac{(y-1)^2}{16(y+3)} \ge0 \]

五、相加并用柯西估计5. Add Them and Use Cauchy

把两个下界相加： Add the two lower bounds:

\[ \frac{2}{x+2}+\frac{1}{y+3} \ge \frac{6-x}{8}+\frac{5-y}{16} \]
\[ = \frac{17-2x-y}{16} \]

接下来由柯西不等式： Next, by Cauchy's inequality:

\[ (2x+y)^2\le (2^2+1^2)(x^2+y^2) \]
\[ (2x+y)^2\le 5\cdot5=25 \]

因为 \(x,y>0\)，所以： Since \(x,y>0\), we get:

\[ 2x+y\le5 \]

因此： Therefore:

\[ \frac{17-2x-y}{16}\ge \frac{17-5}{16}=\frac{12}{16}=\frac34 \]
所以目标式一定不小于 \(\frac34\)。 So the target expression is always at least \(\frac34\).

六、等号成立6. Equality Case

上面几个不等式同时取等号需要： Equality in all the inequalities above requires:

\[ x=2,\qquad y=1 \]

此时： At this point:

\[ x^2+y^2=2^2+1^2=5 \]
\[ \frac{2}{x+2}+\frac{1}{y+3} = \frac{2}{4}+\frac{1}{4} = \frac34 \]
所以最小值确实为 \(\frac34\)。 Therefore, the minimum is indeed \(\frac34\).

七、方法二：用 Engel 形式的柯西不等式7. Method 2: Use the Engel Form of Cauchy's Inequality

把原式写成平方除以正数的形式： Rewrite the expression as sums of squares divided by positive quantities:

\[ \frac{2}{x+2}+\frac{1}{y+3} =\frac{2^2}{2x+4}+\frac{1^2}{y+3} \]

由 Engel 形式的柯西不等式（Titu's Lemma）： By the Engel form of Cauchy's inequality (Titu's Lemma):

\[ \frac{2^2}{2x+4}+\frac{1^2}{y+3} \ge \frac{(2+1)^2}{(2x+4)+(y+3)} =\frac{9}{2x+y+7} \]

第一次不等式取等号条件为： Equality in this first inequality holds when:

\[ \frac{2}{2x+4}=\frac{1}{y+3} \]
\[ 2(y+3)=2x+4 \Longrightarrow x=y+1 \]

再由题设 \(x^2+y^2=5\)，以及柯西不等式： Next, from the condition \(x^2+y^2=5\), apply Cauchy's inequality:

\[ (x^2+y^2)(2^2+1^2)\ge (2x+y)^2 \]
\[ 5\cdot 5\ge (2x+y)^2 \Longrightarrow 2x+y\le 5 \]

因此： Therefore:

\[ \frac{2}{x+2}+\frac{1}{y+3} \ge \frac{9}{2x+y+7} \ge \frac{9}{5+7} =\frac34 \]

若要最终取等号，需要两次不等式同时取等号。第二次柯西不等式取等号条件是 \(x:y=2:1\)，即 \(x=2y\)。与 \(x=y+1\) 联立可得： To achieve the final equality, both inequalities must be equalities. Equality in the second Cauchy inequality requires \(x:y=2:1\), that is, \(x=2y\). Combining this with \(x=y+1\), we get:

\[ x=2, \qquad y=1 \]
\[ \frac{2}{x+2}+\frac{1}{y+3} =\frac{2}{4}+\frac{1}{4} =\frac34 \]
所以用这种方法也可证明：最小值是 \(\frac34\)，当 \(x=2,\ y=1\) 时取到。 So this method also proves that the minimum value is \(\frac34\), attained at \(x=2,\ y=1\).

八、最终答案8. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac34} \]
最终答案：\(\frac34\)，当 \(x=2,\ y=1\) 时取得。Final answer: \(\frac34\), achieved when \(x=2,\ y=1\).
