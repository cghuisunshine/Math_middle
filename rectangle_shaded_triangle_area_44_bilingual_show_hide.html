一、题目1. Problem

长方形面积为 100 cm²，AE=4，FC=3，求阴影三角形 BEF 的面积。The rectangle has area 100 cm², AE=4, FC=3. Find the area of shaded triangle BEF.

三、解法一：坐标法3. Solution 1: Coordinate Method

设长方形宽为 w，高为 h，则 wh=100。Let the rectangle have width w and height h, so wh=100.

B=(0,0), E=(4,h), F=(w,3)

三角形 BEF 面积：Area of triangle BEF:

S = 1/2 × |4×3 - h×w| = 1/2 × |12 - 100| = 44
阴影面积 = 44 cm²Shaded area = 44 cm²

四、解法二：补面积法4. Solution 2: Area Complement Method

用长方形面积减去三个角上的空白三角形。Subtract the three unshaded corner triangles from the rectangle.

S = wh - 1/2·4h - 1/2·3w - 1/2(w-4)(h-3)
S = wh - 2h - 3w/2 - 1/2(wh - 3w - 4h + 12) = wh/2 - 6 = 100/2 - 6 = 44
阴影面积 = 44 cm²Shaded area = 44 cm²

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

S阴影 = 44 cm²
最终答案：44 cm²Final answer: 44 cm²
