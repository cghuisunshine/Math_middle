一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：求根式差的最大值。Original image: find the maximum value of the difference of two radicals.
\[ \text{求 }\sqrt{x^2+25}-\sqrt{(8-x)^2+4}\text{ 的最大值。} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\sqrt{73}} \]
最大值为 \(\sqrt{73}\)，当 \(x=\frac{40}{3}\) 时取得。The maximum is \(\sqrt{73}\), achieved when \(x=\frac{40}{3}\).

三、把根式看成距离3. Interpret the Radicals as Distances

设动点在 \(x\) 轴上：Let the moving point be on the \(x\)-axis:

\[ P=(x,0) \]

取两个定点：Take two fixed points:

\[ A=(0,5),\qquad B=(8,2) \]

那么：Then:

\[ PA=\sqrt{(x-0)^2+(0-5)^2}=\sqrt{x^2+25} \]
\[ PB=\sqrt{(x-8)^2+(0-2)^2}=\sqrt{(8-x)^2+4} \]

所以原式就是：So the expression is:

\[ PA-PB \]

四、几何图示4. Geometric Diagram

由三角形不等式：By the triangle inequality:

\[ PA\le PB+AB \]

移项得到：Rearrange:

\[ PA-PB\le AB \]
图中虚线先表示一般位置的 \(P\)，便于直观看到：任意位置都有 \(PA-PB\le AB\)；而实线表示取到最大值时的特殊位置。The dashed lines show a general position of \(P\), making it visually clear that for any position, \(PA-PB\le AB\). The solid lines show the special position where the maximum is attained.

五、计算最大值5. Compute the Maximum

计算定点 \(A(0,5)\) 与 \(B(8,2)\) 的距离：Compute the distance between \(A(0,5)\) and \(B(8,2)\):

\[ AB=\sqrt{(8-0)^2+(2-5)^2} \]
\[ =\sqrt{64+9} \]
\[ =\sqrt{73} \]

因此：Therefore:

\[ PA-PB\le \sqrt{73} \]

六、什么时候能取到等号？6. When Does Equality Hold?

当 \(A,B,P\) 三点共线，并且 \(B\) 在 \(A\) 和 \(P\) 之间时，有：When \(A,B,P\) are collinear and \(B\) lies between \(A\) and \(P\), we have:

\[ PA=PB+AB \]

于是：Thus:

\[ PA-PB=AB=\sqrt{73} \]

验证这个点确实在 \(x\) 轴上。直线 \(AB\) 的斜率是：Check that such a point really lies on the \(x\)-axis. The slope of line \(AB\) is:

\[ \frac{2-5}{8-0}=-\frac{3}{8} \]

从 \(B(8,2)\) 下降到 \(x\) 轴，需要下降 \(2\)。按斜率 \(-\frac38\)，水平向右走：From \(B(8,2)\) down to the \(x\)-axis, the vertical drop is \(2\). With slope \(-\frac38\), the horizontal move to the right is:

\[ \frac{2}{3/8}=\frac{16}{3} \]

所以：So:

\[ x=8+\frac{16}{3}=\frac{40}{3} \]
这说明等号确实能取到，所以 \(\sqrt{73}\) 就是最大值。This shows the equality case is attainable, so \(\sqrt{73}\) is truly the maximum.

七、最终答案7. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{\sqrt{73}} \]
最大值为 \(\sqrt{73}\)，当 \(x=\frac{40}{3}\) 时取得。The maximum is \(\sqrt{73}\), achieved when \(x=\frac{40}{3}\).
