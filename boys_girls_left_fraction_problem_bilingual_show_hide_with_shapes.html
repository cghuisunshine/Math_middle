一、题目1. Problem

六年2班有 56 人，女生走了 1/3，男生走了 1/4，还剩 40 人。问男女各几人？ Class 6-2 has 56 students. One third of the girls left, one fourth of the boys left, and 40 students remain. How many boys and girls were there?
六年2班有 56 人； 女生走了 1/3； 男生走了 1/4； 还剩 40 人； 问：男生、女生各几人？

三、解法一：设未知数3. Solution 1: Set Variables

设女生有 G 人，男生有 B 人。Let the number of girls be G and the number of boys be B.

G + B = 56

女生走了 1/3，所以女生剩下 2/3；男生走了 1/4，所以男生剩下 3/4。剩下共 40 人：One third of the girls left, so two thirds remain. One fourth of the boys left, so three fourths remain. The remaining total is 40:

(2/3)G + (3/4)B = 40

两边乘以 12：Multiply both sides by 12:

8G + 9B = 480

由 G + B = 56，得：From G + B = 56, we have:

8G + 8B = 448

相减可得：Subtract to get:

B = 32
G = 56 - 32 = 24
男生 32 人，女生 24 人。32 boys and 24 girls.

四、解法二：假设全是女生剩下4. Solution 2: Assume All Were Girls

如果 56 人全部都按女生“剩下 2/3”来算，那么剩下人数应为：If all 56 students were treated as girls, so that 2/3 remain, the remaining number would be:

56 × 2/3 = 112/3

实际剩下 40 人，所以多出来：But the actual remaining total is 40, so the difference is:

40 - 112/3 = 8/3

每把一个“女生”改成“男生”，剩下的人数会增加：Each time we replace a “girl” by a “boy”, the remaining contribution increases by:

3/4 - 2/3 = 1/12

所以男生人数：Therefore the number of boys is:

(8/3) ÷ (1/12) = 32

女生人数：The number of girls is:

56 - 32 = 24
男生 32 人，女生 24 人。32 boys and 24 girls.

五、解法三：图形份数法（3个圆，4个三角形）5. Solution 3: Shape-Parts Method (3 circles, 4 triangles)

把女生平均分成 3 份，所以用 3 个圆表示女生总人数；把男生平均分成 4 份，所以用 4 个三角形表示男生总人数。 Split the girls into 3 equal parts, so use 3 circles to represent all the girls; split the boys into 4 equal parts, so use 4 triangles to represent all the boys.

设 1 个圆表示 x 人，1 个三角形表示 y 人。Let one circle represent x students, and one triangle represent y students.

3x + 4y = 56 2x + 3y = 40

两式相减：Subtract the two equations:

x + y = 16

再用第二个式子：Then use the second equation:

2x + 3y = 40 2(x+y) + y = 40 2×16 + y = 40 y = 8

所以：So:

x = 16 - 8 = 8

女生人数：Girls:

3x = 3×8 = 24

男生人数：Boys:

4y = 4×8 = 32
男生 32 人，女生 24 人。32 boys and 24 girls.

六、最终答案6. Final Answer

男生 = 32 人 女生 = 24 人
最终答案：男生 32 人，女生 24 人。Final answer: 32 boys and 24 girls.
