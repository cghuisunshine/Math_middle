一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：一杯果汁，喝半杯后兑满水；又喝半杯，再兑满水；又喝半杯，再兑满水。问一共喝了多少杯水。 Original image: A cup of juice. Drink half a cup, refill with water; drink half again, refill with water; drink half again, refill with water. How many cups of water were drunk in total?
一杯果汁： 喝了半杯后，兑满水； 又喝了半杯，再兑满水； 又喝了半杯，再兑满水。 问：一共喝了（ ）杯水？

二、核心思路2. Main Idea

只统计“喝进去的水”，不是统计“倒进去的水”。第一次喝的是纯果汁，所以水喝了 0 杯。We count only the water that is actually drunk, not the water poured in. The first drink is pure juice, so no water is drunk then.

三、逐步分析3. Step-by-Step Analysis

步骤Step 杯中成分Contents in cup 这次喝掉的水Water drunk this time
开始Start 1 杯果汁，0 杯水1 cup juice, 0 cup water 0

第二次兑满水后After second refill 1/4 果汁 + 3/4 水1/4 juice + 3/4 water —
第三次喝半杯Third half-cup drink 喝掉其中一半drink half of the mixture 3/8

四、计算总共喝了多少水4. Calculate the Total Water Drunk

总共喝的水 = 0 + 1/4 + 3/8 = 2/8 + 3/8 = 5/8
最后一次“再兑满水”只是倒入水，还没有喝，所以不计入“喝了多少水”。 The final refill is only water poured into the cup, not water drunk, so it is not counted.
答案：5/8 杯水Answer: 5/8 cup of water

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

一共喝了 5/8 杯水
最终答案：5/8Final answer: 5/8
