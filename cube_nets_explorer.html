How to use:
Click Fold net to watch the selected 2D net close into a cube. Click Unfold net to open it again. You can also use Next fold step and Previous fold step to study one hinge movement at a time. When the cube is fully formed, use Up to rotate horizontally and Down to rotate vertically for a different view.
