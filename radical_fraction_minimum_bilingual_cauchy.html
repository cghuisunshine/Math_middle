一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[ x>0,\qquad y>0 \]
\[ \text{求 }\frac{x+\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}}{2x+3y}\text{ 的最小值} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac45} \]
最小值为 \(\frac45\)，当 \(4x=9y\) 时取得。The minimum is \(\frac45\), achieved when \(4x=9y\).

三、方法一：直接证明不小于 \(\frac45\)3. Method 1: Directly Prove It Is at Least \(\frac45\)

因为 \(x>0,y>0\)，所以分母 \(2x+3y>0\)。只需证明：Since \(x>0,y>0\), the denominator \(2x+3y>0\). It is enough to prove:

\[ \frac{x+\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}}{2x+3y}\ge \frac45 \]

两边同乘正数 \(5(2x+3y)\)：Multiply both sides by the positive number \(5(2x+3y)\):

\[ 5x+5\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}\ge 8x+12y \]
\[ 5\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}\ge 3x+12y \]

两边都为正，可以平方：Both sides are positive, so we may square:

\[ 25(x^2+9y^2)\ge (3x+12y)^2 \]
\[ 25x^2+225y^2\ge 9x^2+72xy+144y^2 \]
\[ 16x^2-72xy+81y^2\ge0 \]
\[ (4x-9y)^2\ge0 \]
因为平方恒不小于 \(0\)，所以原式一定不小于 \(\frac45\)。Since a square is always nonnegative, the expression is always at least \(\frac45\).

四、等号成立条件4. Equality Condition

\[ (4x-9y)^2=0 \]
\[ 4x=9y \]

例如取 \(y=4\)，则 \(x=9\)，代入原式：For example, take \(y=4\), then \(x=9\). Substitute:

\[ \frac{9+\sqrt{9^2+9\cdot4^2}}{2\cdot9+3\cdot4} = \frac{9+\sqrt{81+144}}{18+12} = \frac{9+15}{30} = \frac45 \]
所以最小值可以取到。So the minimum is attainable.

五、方法二：齐次式，设比例5. Method 2: Homogeneous Expression, Use a Ratio

分子和分母都是一次齐次式，所以原式只与 \(x:y\) 的比例有关。Both numerator and denominator are homogeneous of degree 1, so the expression only depends on the ratio \(x:y\).

令 \(t=3y\)，原式变为：Let \(t=3y\). The expression becomes:

\[ \frac{x+\sqrt{x^2+t^2}}{2x+t} \]

再令Let

\[ r=\frac{x}{t}=\frac{x}{3y}>0 \]

原式变为：The expression becomes:

\[ f(r)=\frac{r+\sqrt{r^2+1}}{2r+1} \]

由等号条件 \(4x=9y=3(3y)=3t\)，得：From the equality condition \(4x=9y=3(3y)=3t\), we get:

\[ r=\frac{x}{t}=\frac34 \]

代入：Substitute:

\[ f\left(\frac34\right) = \frac{\frac34+\sqrt{\frac{9}{16}+1}}{2\cdot\frac34+1} = \frac{\frac34+\frac54}{\frac32+1} = \frac{2}{\frac52} = \frac45 \]

六、方法三：先设最小值 \(k\)，反推柯西系数6. Method 3: Set the Minimum as \(k\), Then Reverse-Engineer the Cauchy Coefficients

诀窍：不要一开始就猜 \(\frac35,\frac45\)。先设最小值是 \(k\)，再反推柯西不等式里需要的系数。 Key trick: Do not guess \(\frac35,\frac45\) at the beginning. First assume the minimum is \(k\), then reverse-engineer the coefficients needed in Cauchy's inequality.

原式为： The expression is:

\[ E=\frac{x+\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}}{2x+3y} \]

我们希望证明： We want to prove:

\[ \frac{x+\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}}{2x+3y}\ge k \]

因为 \(2x+3y>0\)，所以等价于： Since \(2x+3y>0\), this is equivalent to:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}\ge k(2x+3y) \]

移项： Rearrange:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9y^2}\ge (2k-1)x+3ky \]

注意根号部分： Notice the radical part:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9y^2}=\sqrt{x^2+(3y)^2} \]

它是向量 \((x,3y)\) 的长度。柯西不等式告诉我们，只要 \(u^2+v^2=1\)，就有： It is the length of the vector \((x,3y)\). Cauchy's inequality tells us that if \(u^2+v^2=1\), then:

\[ ux+v(3y)\le \sqrt{u^2+v^2}\sqrt{x^2+(3y)^2} =\sqrt{x^2+(3y)^2} \]

所以我们希望右边刚好变成： So we want the right side to match:

\[ (2k-1)x+3ky \]

于是需要： Therefore we need:

\[ u=2k-1,\qquad v=k \]

同时柯西要求 \(u^2+v^2=1\)，所以： At the same time, Cauchy requires \(u^2+v^2=1\), so:

\[ (2k-1)^2+k^2=1 \]

展开： Expand:

\[ 4k^2-4k+1+k^2=1 \]
\[ 5k^2-4k=0 \]
\[ k(5k-4)=0 \]

因为最小值应为正数，所以： Since the minimum should be positive:

\[ k=\frac45 \]

于是反推出： Then we get:

\[ u=2k-1=2\cdot\frac45-1=\frac35 \]
\[ v=k=\frac45 \]

因此构造出柯西不等式： Therefore we construct the Cauchy inequality:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+(3y)^2} \ge \frac35x+\frac45(3y) \]
\[ \sqrt{x^2+9y^2} \ge \frac35x+\frac{12}{5}y \]

所以： Thus:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+9y^2} \ge x+\frac35x+\frac{12}{5}y \]
\[ = \frac85x+\frac{12}{5}y = \frac45(2x+3y) \]

两边除以正数 \(2x+3y\)： Divide both sides by the positive number \(2x+3y\):

\[ \frac{x+\sqrt{x^2+9y^2}}{2x+3y}\ge \frac45 \]

等号条件Equality Condition

柯西取等号时，向量 \((x,3y)\) 与 \((u,v)=\left(\frac35,\frac45\right)\) 同方向： Equality in Cauchy occurs when \((x,3y)\) is in the same direction as \((u,v)=\left(\frac35,\frac45\right)\):

\[ (x,3y)=\lambda\left(\frac35,\frac45\right) \]

所以： So:

\[ \frac{x}{3y}=\frac{\frac35}{\frac45}=\frac34 \]
\[ 4x=9y \]
因此最小值为 \(\boxed{\frac45}\)，当 \(4x=9y\) 时取得。 Therefore the minimum is \(\boxed{\frac45}\), achieved when \(4x=9y\).

七、最终答案7. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac45} \]
最终答案：\(\boxed{\frac45}\)，当 \(4x=9y\) 时取得。Final answer: \(\boxed{\frac45}\), achieved when \(4x=9y\).
