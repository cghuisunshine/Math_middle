一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：比较 \(25^{24}\) 和 \(24^{25}\) 的大小。 Original image: Compare \(25^{24}\) and \(24^{25}\).
\[ 25^{24} \; \bigcirc \; 24^{25} \]

二、解法一：作商比较2. Solution 1: Compare by ratio

两个数都是正数，所以比较它们的商：Both numbers are positive, so compare their ratio:

\[ R=\frac{25^{24}}{24^{25}} \]

把分母写成 \(24^{24}\times 24\)：Write the denominator as \(24^{24}\times 24\):

\[ R=\frac{25^{24}}{24^{24}\cdot 24} =\left(\frac{25}{24}\right)^{24}\cdot\frac1{24} =\frac{\left(1+\frac1{24}\right)^{24}}{24} \]

利用常见估计：Use the common estimate:

\[ \left(1+\frac1n\right)^n

所以：So:

\[ \left(1+\frac1{24}\right)^{24}<3 \]

代回 \(R\)：Substitute back into \(R\):

\[ R<\frac3{24}=\frac18<1 \]
\[ \frac{25^{24}}{24^{25}}<1 \quad\Longrightarrow\quad 25^{24}<24^{25} \]
所以 \(24^{25}\) 更大。So \(24^{25}\) is greater.

三、解法二：用对数近似3. Solution 2: Use logarithms

比较两个幂，可以比较它们的自然对数：To compare the powers, compare their natural logarithms:

\[ 25^{24} \text{ 与 } 24^{25} \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad 24\ln25 \text{ 与 } 25\ln24 \]
\[ \ln25\approx3.2189,\qquad \ln24\approx3.1781 \]
\[ 24\ln25\approx77.25,\qquad 25\ln24\approx79.45 \]
\[ 25\ln24>24\ln25 \quad\Longrightarrow\quad 24^{25}>25^{24} \]
所以 \(24^{25}\) 更大。So \(24^{25}\) is greater.

四、解法三：连乘积比较法4. Solution 3: Product comparison method

解法三图：把 \(\left(\frac{25}{24}\right)^{24}\) 看成 24 个相同因子的乘积，再与一组逐渐变大的因子比较。 Solution 3 image: View \(\left(\frac{25}{24}\right)^{24}\) as 24 identical factors, then compare with a telescoping product.

仍然先作商：Again, compare the ratio:

\[ R=\frac{25^{24}}{24^{25}} =\frac1{24}\left(\frac{25}{24}\right)^{24} \]

把其中 23 个 \(\frac{25}{24}\) 拿出来比较：Compare 23 of the factors \(\frac{25}{24}\):

\[ \left(\frac{25}{24}\right)^{23} = \left(1+\frac1{24}\right)^{23} \]

因为下面这些因子都不小于 \(1+\frac1{24}\)：The following factors are all at least \(1+\frac1{24}\):

\[ 1+\frac1{24},\; 1+\frac1{23},\; 1+\frac1{22},\; \ldots,\; 1+\frac12 \]

所以：Therefore:

\[ \left(1+\frac1{24}\right)^{23} < \left(1+\frac1{24}\right) \left(1+\frac1{23}\right) \left(1+\frac1{22}\right) \cdots \left(1+\frac12\right) \]

右边可以连乘约分：The right side telescopes:

\[ \frac{25}{24}\cdot\frac{24}{23}\cdot\frac{23}{22}\cdots\frac32 = \frac{25}{2} \]

于是：Thus:

\[ \left(\frac{25}{24}\right)^{23}<\frac{25}{2} \]

代回原来的商：Substitute back into the original ratio:

\[ R = \frac1{24}\cdot\frac{25}{24}\cdot \left(\frac{25}{24}\right)^{23} < \frac1{24}\cdot\frac{25}{24}\cdot\frac{25}{2} = \frac{625}{1152} <1 \]
\[ R<1 \quad\Longrightarrow\quad 25^{24}<24^{25} \]
所以 \(24^{25}\) 更大。So \(24^{25}\) is greater.

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

\[ 25^{24}<24^{25} \]
