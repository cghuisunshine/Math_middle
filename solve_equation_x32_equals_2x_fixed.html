一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：解方程 \(x^{32}=2^x\)。Original image: Solve \(x^{32}=2^x\).
\[ x^{32}=2^x \]

二、常见整数解答案2. Common Integer-Solution Answer

\[ \boxed{x=256} \]
如果题目默认求正整数解，则答案是 \(256\)。If the problem asks for a positive integer solution, the answer is \(256\).

三、方法一：设 \(x=2^a\)3. Method 1: Set \(x=2^a\)

因为右边是 \(2^x\)，所以自然设：Since the right side is \(2^x\), we naturally set:

\[ x=2^a \]

那么：Then:

\[ x^{32}=(2^a)^{32}=2^{32a} \]

原方程变为：The equation becomes:

\[ 2^{32a}=2^x \]

底数相同，所以指数相等：The bases are the same, so the exponents are equal:

\[ 32a=x \]

又因为 \(x=2^a\)，所以：Since \(x=2^a\), we also have:

\[ 2^a=32a \]

如果求整数解，试整数 \(a\)。当 \(a=8\) 时：For integer solutions, try integer values of \(a\). When \(a=8\):

\[ 2^8=256,\qquad 32\times 8=256 \]

所以 \(a=8\) 成立，于是：So \(a=8\) works, hence:

\[ x=2^a=2^8=256 \]

四、方法二：取 \(\frac1x\) 次方的速解法4. Method 2: A Quick Approach by Taking the \(\frac1x\)-th Power

补充板书方法。Supplementary board method.

从原方程出发：Start from the original equation:

\[ x^{32}=2^x \]

两边同时取 \(\frac1x\) 次方：Take the \(\frac1x\)-th power on both sides:

\[ (x^{32})^{1/x}=(2^x)^{1/x} \]

右边化简为 \(2\)：The right-hand side simplifies to \(2\):

\[ (x^{32})^{1/x}=2 \]

左边可以写成：The left-hand side can be rewritten as:

\[ (x^{1/x})^{32}=2 \]

于是：Therefore:

\[ x^{1/x}=2^{1/32} \]

注意：Notice:

\[ 2^{1/32}=2^{8/256}=(2^8)^{1/256}=256^{1/256} \]

所以 \(x=256\) 正好满足：So \(x=256\) satisfies:

\[ x^{1/x}=256^{1/256} \]

因此得到：Therefore:

\[ \boxed{x=256} \]
这个方法很适合快速看出整数解 \(256\)。如果要做严格的整数证明，方法一更完整。This is a quick way to spot the integer solution \(256\). For a more rigorous integer proof, Method 1 is more complete.

五、直接验证5. Direct Check

\[ 256^{32}=(2^8)^{32}=2^{256} \]
\[ 2^x=2^{256} \]

左右相等，所以：Both sides are equal, so:

\[ \boxed{x=256} \]

六、补充：如果求所有实数解6. Extra: If All Real Solutions Are Required

如果题目没有限制 \(x\) 是正整数，那么实数解不止一个。除了 \(x=256\)，还有两个近似实数解。If the problem does not restrict \(x\) to positive integers, there is more than one real solution. Besides \(x=256\), there are two additional approximate real solutions.

由 \(x^{32}=2^x\)，取对数可以得到：Taking logarithms in \(x^{32}=2^x\) gives:

\[ 32\ln |x|=x\ln 2 \]

数值上，实数解约为：Numerically, the real solutions are approximately:

\[ x\approx -0.979017,\qquad x\approx 1.022393,\qquad x=256 \]
