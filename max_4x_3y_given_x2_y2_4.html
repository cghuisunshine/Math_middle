二、解法一：柯西不等式2. Solution 1: Cauchy's Inequality

把 \(4x+3y\) 看成两个向量的点积：View \(4x+3y\) as the dot product of two vectors:

\[ 4x+3y=(4,3)\cdot(x,y) \]

由柯西不等式：By Cauchy's inequality:

\[ 4x+3y\le \sqrt{4^2+3^2}\sqrt{x^2+y^2} \]
\[ =\sqrt{25}\cdot\sqrt4=5\cdot2=10 \]

当 \((x,y)\) 与 \((4,3)\) 同方向时取等号。Equality holds when \((x,y)\) has the same direction as \((4,3)\).

最大值：10Maximum value: 10

三、等号什么时候成立？3. When Equality Holds

同方向表示：Same direction means:

\[ (x,y)=k(4,3) \]

代入 \(x^2+y^2=4\)：Substitute into \(x^2+y^2=4\):

\[ (4k)^2+(3k)^2=4 \]
\[ 25k^2=4,\qquad k=\frac25 \]

所以：So:

\[ x=\frac85,\qquad y=\frac65 \]

检验：Check:

\[ 4x+3y=4\cdot\frac85+3\cdot\frac65 =\frac{32}{5}+\frac{18}{5}=10 \]

五、解法二：只用 \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\)5. Solution 2: Using Only \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\)

设：Let:

\[ S=4x+3y \]

先平方：Square it:

\[ S^2=(4x+3y)^2=16x^2+24xy+9y^2 \]

只用 \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\)。取 \(a=3x,\ b=4y\)，则：Use only \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\). Take \(a=3x,\ b=4y\), then:

\[ 9x^2+16y^2\ge 24xy \]

所以：So:

\[ S^2 \le 16x^2+9y^2+(9x^2+16y^2) \]
\[ =25x^2+25y^2=25(x^2+y^2)=25\cdot4=100 \]
\[ S\le10 \]
