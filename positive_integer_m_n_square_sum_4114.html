一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：\(m^2+n^2=4114\)，且 \(m,n\) 是正整数，求 \(m+n\)。Original image: \(m^2+n^2=4114\), where \(m,n\) are positive integers. Find \(m+n\).
\[ m^2+n^2=4114,\qquad m,n\in \mathbb{Z}^+，\qquad \text{求 }m+n. \]

二、先分解 41142. Factor 4114

\[ 4114=2\times2057 \]
\[ 2057=17\times121=17\times11^2 \]
\[ 4114=2\times17\times11^2=34\times11^2 \]

三、把 34 写成两个平方和3. Write 34 as a Sum of Two Squares

\[ 34=25+9=5^2+3^2 \]

所以：Therefore:

\[ 4114=11^2(5^2+3^2) \]
\[ =(11\cdot5)^2+(11\cdot3)^2 \]
\[ =55^2+33^2 \]

四、得到正整数解4. Get the Positive Integer Solutions

因为 \(m,n\) 是正整数，所以可以取：Since \(m,n\) are positive integers, we can take:

\[ (m,n)=(55,33)\quad \text{或}\quad (33,55) \]

无论顺序如何，和相同：In either order, the sum is the same:

\[ m+n=55+33=88 \]
答案：88Answer: 88

五、快速验证5. Quick Check

\[ 55^2=3025,\qquad 33^2=1089 \]
\[ 3025+1089=4114 \]
\[ 55+33=88 \]
关键：题目必须说明 \(m,n\) 是正整数，否则只知道 \(m^2+n^2=4114\) 时，\(m+n\) 不唯一。Key point: the condition that \(m,n\) are positive integers is necessary; otherwise \(m+n\) is not unique.

六、最终答案6. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{88} \]
