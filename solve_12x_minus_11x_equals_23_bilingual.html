一、题目1. Problem

题目原图 Original problem image
\[ 12^x-11^x=23 \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{x=2} \]
方程的解是 \(x=2\)。 The solution is \(x=2\).

三、核心观察：23 可以写成 \(12+11\)3. Key Observation: \(23\) Can Be Written as \(12+11\)

右边是： The right-hand side is:

\[ 23=12+11 \]

而左边是 \(12^x-11^x\)。看到相邻整数 \(12,11\) 和它们的和 \(23\)，很自然想到平方差公式。 The left-hand side is \(12^x-11^x\). Seeing the consecutive integers \(12,11\) and their sum \(23\), it is natural to think of the difference of squares.

\[ a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b) \]

四、尝试 \(x=2\)4. Try \(x=2\)

当 \(x=2\) 时： When \(x=2\):

\[ 12^x-11^x=12^2-11^2 \]
\[ =(12-11)(12+11) \]
\[ =1\times23=23 \]
所以 \(x=2\) 满足原方程。 So \(x=2\) satisfies the original equation.

五、为什么自然想到 \(x=2\)5. Why \(x=2\) Is Natural

因为： Because:

\[ 12-11=1 \]
\[ 12^2-11^2=(12-11)(12+11) \]
\[ =12+11=23 \]
所以看到 \(23\) 和相邻整数 \(12,11\)，最自然就是尝试平方差，也就是 \(x=2\)。 So when we see \(23\) together with the consecutive numbers \(12\) and \(11\), the most natural idea is the difference of squares, meaning \(x=2\).

六、唯一性说明6. Why This Solution Is Unique

如果只在正整数范围内解，验证 \(x=2\) 已经足够。但若把它看作实数方程，也可以说明它只有这个正解。 If we solve only over positive integers, checking \(x=2\) is enough. If we view it as a real equation, we can also explain why this positive solution is unique.

当 \(x\ge0\) 时，\(12^x\) 比 \(11^x\) 增长更快，因此 \(12^x-11^x\) 随 \(x\) 增大而增大；而当 \(x<0\) 时，\(12^x-11^x<0\)，不可能等于 \(23\)。 For \(x\ge0\), \(12^x\) grows faster than \(11^x\), so \(12^x-11^x\) increases as \(x\) increases. For \(x<0\), \(12^x-11^x<0\), so it cannot equal \(23\).

因此实数范围内也只有 \(x=2\) 这个解。 Therefore, over the real numbers, \(x=2\) is also the only solution.
