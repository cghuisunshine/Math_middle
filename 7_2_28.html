圆柱体积应用题讲解 Cylinder Volume Word Problems

第 2 题 Problem 2

一只圆柱形桶内装满了水，已知桶的底面直径为 \(a\)，高为 \(b\)。 又知另一个长方体形容器的长为 \(b\)，宽为 \(a\)。 如果把这只圆柱形桶中的水全部倒入这个长方体形容器中，水不溢出， 那么水面的高度是多少？ A cylindrical bucket is filled with water. Its base diameter is \(a\), and its height is \(b\). Another rectangular container has length \(b\) and width \(a\). If all the water from the cylindrical bucket is poured into the rectangular container without overflowing, what is the height of the water?
圆柱形桶的底面直径是 \(a\)，所以半径是：The cylindrical bucket has base diameter \(a\), so its radius is: \[ r=\frac a2 \]
圆柱桶中水的体积为：The volume of water in the cylinder is: \[ V=\pi r^2h \] 代入 \(r=\frac a2\)，高为 \(b\)：Substitute \(r=\frac a2\) and height \(b\): \[ V=\pi\left(\frac a2\right)^2b \] \[ V=\frac{\pi a^2b}{4} \]
长方体容器的底面积为：The base area of the rectangular container is: \[ S=b\times a=ab \]
水面的高度等于水的体积除以底面积：The water height equals volume divided by base area: \[ h=\frac{V}{S} \] \[ h=\frac{\frac{\pi a^2b}{4}}{ab} \] \[ h=\frac{\pi a}{4} \]
答案：Answer:\[ \boxed{\frac{\pi a}{4}} \]

第 3 题 Problem 3

如图，单位：cm。图（1）的瓶子中盛满了水。 如果将这个瓶子中的水全部倒入图（2）这样的杯子中， 那么一共需要多少个这样的杯子？ As shown, all measurements are in cm. The bottle in figure (1) is filled with water. If all the water is poured into cups like the one in figure (2), how many such cups are needed in total?
瓶子由上下两个圆柱组成。The bottle is made of two stacked cylinders.
下半部分圆柱的直径是 \(2a\)，所以半径是：The lower cylinder has diameter \(2a\), so its radius is: \[ r=a \] 高为 \(H\)，所以体积为：Its height is \(H\), so its volume is: \[ V_1=\pi a^2H \]
上半部分圆柱的直径是 \(a\)，所以半径是：The upper cylinder has diameter \(a\), so its radius is: \[ r=\frac a2 \] 高为 \(h\)，所以体积为：Its height is \(h\), so its volume is: \[ V_2=\pi\left(\frac a2\right)^2h \] \[ V_2=\frac{\pi a^2h}{4} \]
因此，瓶子中水的总体积为：Therefore, the total volume of water in the bottle is: \[ V=\pi a^2H+\frac{\pi a^2h}{4} \]
杯子的直径是：The cup diameter is: \[ \frac12a \] 所以杯子的半径是：So the cup radius is: \[ \frac14a \] 杯子的高是 \(8\)，所以一个杯子的体积为：The cup height is \(8\), so the volume of one cup is: \[ V_{\text{cup}}=\pi\left(\frac a4\right)^2\cdot 8 \] \[ V_{\text{cup}}=\frac{\pi a^2}{2} \]
需要的杯子数量为：The number of cups needed is: \[ \frac{V}{V_{\text{cup}}} \] \[ = \frac{\pi a^2H+\frac{\pi a^2h}{4}} {\frac{\pi a^2}{2}} \] \[ = 2H+\frac h2 \]
答案：Answer:\[ \boxed{2H+\frac h2} \]

如果题目要求“必须是整数个杯子，且不能溢出”，则需要向上取整：If the number of cups must be an integer and there can be no overflow, round up:

\[ \boxed{\left\lceil 2H+\frac h2 \right\rceil} \]
