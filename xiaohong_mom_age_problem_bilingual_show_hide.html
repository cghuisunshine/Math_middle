一、题目1. Problem

3年前妈妈年龄是小红的3倍，5年后两人共104岁，今年小红几岁？ Three years ago, Mom was 3 times Xiaohong's age. Five years later, their total age will be 104. How old is Xiaohong now?
3年前妈妈年龄是小红的3倍； 5年后两人共104岁； 问：今年小红几岁？

二、时间线理解2. Timeline

三、解法一：设未知数3. Solution 1: Set Variables

设今年小红 x 岁，妈妈 M 岁。Let Xiaohong be x years old now, and Mom be M years old now.

由“3年前妈妈年龄是小红的3倍”：From “three years ago, Mom was 3 times Xiaohong's age”:

M - 3 = 3(x - 3) M - 3 = 3x - 9 M = 3x - 6

由“5年后两人共104岁”：From “five years later, their total age will be 104”:

(M + 5) + (x + 5) = 104 M + x = 94

代入 M=3x-6：Substitute M=3x-6:

(3x - 6) + x = 94 4x = 100 x = 25
今年小红 25 岁。Xiaohong is 25 years old now.

四、解法二：年龄和倒推4. Solution 2: Work Back from the Sum

从3年前到5年后，相隔8年。两个人都长大8岁，所以年龄和增加：From 3 years ago to 5 years later is 8 years. Both people get 8 years older, so their total age increases by:

8 + 8 = 16

所以3年前两人年龄和是：Therefore, their total age 3 years ago was:

104 - 16 = 88

3年前妈妈是小红的3倍，两人年龄和就是小红年龄的4倍：Three years ago Mom was 3 times Xiaohong's age, so together they were 4 parts:

小红3年前年龄 = 88 ÷ 4 = 22

今年小红：Now Xiaohong is:

22 + 3 = 25
今年小红 25 岁。Xiaohong is 25 years old now.

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

今年小红 = 25 岁
最终答案：25 岁Final answer: 25 years old
