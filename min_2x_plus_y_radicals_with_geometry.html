一、题目1. Problem

题目原图 Original problem image
\[ \sqrt{2x+1}+\sqrt{y+2}=4 \]
\[ \text{求 }2x+y\text{ 的最小值。} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[\boxed{5}\]
最小值为 \(5\)，当 \(x=\frac32,\ y=2\) 时取得。 The minimum is \(5\), achieved when \(x=\frac32,\ y=2\).

三、方法一：代数换元法3. Method 1: Algebraic Substitution

令：Let:

\[ a=\sqrt{2x+1},\qquad b=\sqrt{y+2} \]

则由题意：Then from the condition:

\[ a+b=4 \]

并且：Also:

\[ 2x=a^2-1,\qquad y=b^2-2 \]

所以目标式变成：So the target expression becomes:

\[ 2x+y=(a^2-1)+(b^2-2)=a^2+b^2-3 \]

当 \(a+b=4\) 固定时，\(a^2+b^2\) 最小时有 \(a=b=2\)。When \(a+b=4\) is fixed, \(a^2+b^2\) is minimized when \(a=b=2\).

\[ a^2+b^2\ge \frac{(a+b)^2}2=\frac{16}2=8 \]
\[ 2x+y=a^2+b^2-3\ge 8-3=5 \]

等号成立时：At equality:

\[ a=b=2 \]
\[ \sqrt{2x+1}=2,\qquad \sqrt{y+2}=2 \]
\[ x=\frac32,\qquad y=2 \]
因此最小值为 \(\boxed{5}\)。 Therefore, the minimum value is \(\boxed{5}\).

四、方法二：图示几何证法4. Method 2: Geometric Diagram Proof

图示几何证法：把两个直角三角形拼接起来。 Geometric proof: combine two right triangles.

1）先看两个直角三角形的斜边1) Look at the hypotenuse of the two right triangles

左边直角三角形的两条直角边分别是 \(\sqrt{2x+1}\) 和 \(\sqrt{y+2}\)。因此它的斜边长为： In the left right triangle, the two legs are \(\sqrt{2x+1}\) and \(\sqrt{y+2}\). Hence its hypotenuse is:

\[ \sqrt{(\sqrt{2x+1})^2+(\sqrt{y+2})^2} =\sqrt{2x+1+y+2} =\sqrt{2x+y+3} \]

右边直角三角形同理，斜边也等于： Similarly, the right triangle on the other side has hypotenuse:

\[ \sqrt{2x+y+3} \]

2）这两条斜边互相垂直2) These two hypotenuses are perpendicular

图中间标出了直角，所以这两条长度都为 \(\sqrt{2x+y+3}\) 的线段构成一个等腰直角三角形的两条直角边。 The central right angle in the picture shows that the two equal segments of length \(\sqrt{2x+y+3}\) are the legs of an isosceles right triangle.

因此上方白色线段（大三角形的斜边）长度为： Therefore, the top white segment (the hypotenuse of the larger isosceles right triangle) has length:

\[ L=\sqrt2\,\sqrt{2x+y+3} \]

3）再从坐标差的角度看这条上边3) Compute the same top segment another way

这条上边连接左右两个顶点。它的水平距离等于底边两段之和： This top segment joins the left and right top vertices. Its horizontal displacement equals the sum of the two bottom segments:

\[ \sqrt{2x+1}+\sqrt{y+2}=4 \]

它的竖直高度差等于： Its vertical difference equals:

\[ \sqrt{2x+1}-\sqrt{y+2} \]

所以根据勾股定理：So by the Pythagorean theorem:

\[ L^2=\left(\sqrt{2x+1}+\sqrt{y+2}\right)^2+ \left(\sqrt{2x+1}-\sqrt{y+2}\right)^2 \]
\[ L^2=4^2+\left(\sqrt{2x+1}-\sqrt{y+2}\right)^2\ge 16 \]
\[ L\ge 4 \]

4）联立两种表示，得到下界4) Compare the two expressions for the same segment

\[ \sqrt2\,\sqrt{2x+y+3}=L\ge 4 \]
\[ \sqrt{2x+y+3}\ge 2\sqrt2 \]
\[ 2x+y+3\ge 8 \]
\[ 2x+y\ge 5 \]

5）什么时候取等号？5) When does equality hold?

要使 \(L=4\)，必须有：For \(L=4\), we must have:

\[ \sqrt{2x+1}-\sqrt{y+2}=0 \]
\[ \sqrt{2x+1}=\sqrt{y+2} \]

再结合：Together with:

\[ \sqrt{2x+1}+\sqrt{y+2}=4 \]

得到两者都等于 \(2\)。于是：we get both are equal to \(2\). Hence:

\[ 2x+1=4\Rightarrow x=\frac32 \]
\[ y+2=4\Rightarrow y=2 \]
所以图示几何法也得到：\(2x+y\) 的最小值是 \(\boxed{5}\)。 So the geometric method also gives the minimum value \(\boxed{5}\).
