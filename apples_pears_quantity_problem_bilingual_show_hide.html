一、题目1. Problem

苹果比梨的一半多 12 个；梨比苹果多 6 个；苹果有多少个？Apples are 12 more than half of the pears; pears are 6 more than apples. How many apples are there?
苹果比梨的一半多 12 个； 梨比苹果多 6 个； 问：苹果有多少个？

二、解法一：设未知数2. Solution 1: Set variables

设苹果有 A 个，梨有 P 个。Let apples be A and pears be P.

A = P/2 + 12 P = A + 6

把 P=A+6 代入：Substitute P=A+6:

A = (A+6)/2 + 12 2A = A + 6 + 24 2A = A + 30 A = 30
苹果有 30 个。There are 30 apples.

四、解法二：直接代换4. Solution 2: Direct substitution

梨比苹果多 6 个，所以“梨的一半”就是：Pears are 6 more than apples, so “half of the pears” is:

(苹果 + 6)/2

苹果比梨的一半多 12 个：Apples are 12 more than half of pears:

苹果 = (苹果+6)/2 + 12 苹果/2 = 15 苹果 = 30
苹果有 30 个。There are 30 apples.

五、最终答案5. Final answer

苹果 = 30 个
最终答案：30 个Final answer: 30 apples
