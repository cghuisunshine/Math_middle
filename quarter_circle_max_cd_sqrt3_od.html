一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：\(OA=6,\ OA\perp OB,\ CD\perp OB\)，求 \(CD+\sqrt3OD\) 的最大值。Original image: \(OA=6,\ OA\perp OB,\ CD\perp OB\). Find the maximum of \(CD+\sqrt3OD\).
\[OA=6,\quad OA\perp OB,\quad CD\perp OB\]\[\text{求 }CD+\sqrt3\,OD\text{ 的最大值}\]

二、关键观察2. Key Observation

图中 \(C\) 在以 \(O\) 为圆心、半径为 \(6\) 的四分之一圆弧上，所以：Point \(C\) lies on a quarter circle centered at \(O\) with radius \(6\), so:

\[OC=6\]

又因为 \(CD\perp OB\)，所以 \(\triangle OCD\) 是直角三角形，直角边是 \(OD\) 和 \(CD\)。Since \(CD\perp OB\), triangle \(OCD\) is right, with legs \(OD\) and \(CD\).

\[OD^2+CD^2=OC^2=36\]

三、解法一：设两条直角边，用柯西不等式3. Solution 1: Cauchy's Inequality

设：Let:

\[OD=x,\qquad CD=y\]

由勾股定理：By the Pythagorean theorem:

\[x^2+y^2=36\]

要求最大值：We want to maximize:

\[y+\sqrt3x\]

由柯西不等式：By Cauchy's inequality:

\[y+\sqrt3x\le\sqrt{y^2+x^2}\sqrt{1^2+(\sqrt3)^2}=\sqrt{36}\cdot\sqrt4=12\]

等号成立时 \(x:y=\sqrt3:1\)，即 \(OD=3\sqrt3,\ CD=3\)。Equality holds when \(x:y=\sqrt3:1\), i.e. \(OD=3\sqrt3,\ CD=3\).

最大值：12Maximum value: 12

五、解法二：三角函数5. Solution 2: Trigonometry

设 \(\angle BOC=\theta\)，则：Let \(\angle BOC=\theta\). Then:

\[OD=6\cos\theta,\qquad CD=6\sin\theta\]
\[CD+\sqrt3OD=6\sin\theta+6\sqrt3\cos\theta=6(\sin\theta+\sqrt3\cos\theta)\]
\[\sin\theta+\sqrt3\cos\theta\le\sqrt{1^2+(\sqrt3)^2}\sqrt{\sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta}=2\]
\[CD+\sqrt3OD\le 12\]
最大值：12Maximum value: 12

六、解法三：只用 \(a^2+b^2\ge 2ab\)6. Solution 3: Using Only \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\)

仍设 \(OD=x,\ CD=y\)，则 \(x^2+y^2=36\)。令：Again let \(OD=x,\ CD=y\), so \(x^2+y^2=36\). Let:

\[S=y+\sqrt3x\]

平方：Square it:

\[S^2=y^2+3x^2+2\sqrt3xy\]

只用 \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\)，取 \(a=\sqrt3y,\ b=x\)：Use only \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\), taking \(a=\sqrt3y,\ b=x\):

\[3y^2+x^2\ge2\sqrt3xy\]
\[S^2\le y^2+3x^2+(3y^2+x^2)=4(x^2+y^2)=144\]
\[S\le12\]
最大值：12Maximum value: 12
