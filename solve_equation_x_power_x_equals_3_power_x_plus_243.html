一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：解方程 \(x^x=3^{x+243}\)。Original image: Solve \(x^x=3^{x+243}\).
\[ x^x=3^{x+243} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{x=81} \]
方程的解是 \(x=81\)。The solution is \(x=81\).

三、核心想法：把 \(x\) 设成 \(3\) 的幂3. Key Idea: Set \(x\) as a Power of \(3\)

右边是 \(3\) 的幂，所以自然设：The right side is a power of \(3\), so naturally set:

\[ x=3^a \]

这样左边可以化成同底数：Then the left side can be rewritten with the same base:

\[ x^x=(3^a)^{3^a}=3^{a\cdot 3^a} \]

右边为：The right side is:

\[ 3^{x+243}=3^{3^a+243} \]

四、比较指数4. Compare the Exponents

因为两边底数都是 \(3\)，所以指数相等：Since both sides have base \(3\), their exponents must be equal:

\[ a\cdot 3^a=3^a+243 \]

移项：Rearrange:

\[ a\cdot 3^a-3^a=243 \]
\[ (a-1)3^a=243 \]

又因为：Also:

\[ 243=3^5 \]

现在试整数 \(a\)：Now try integer \(a\):

\[ a=4 \]

因为：Because:

\[ (a-1)3^a=(4-1)3^4=3\cdot 81=243 \]

所以：Therefore:

\[ a=4 \]

五、求出 \(x\)5. Find \(x\)

由 \(x=3^a\)，且 \(a=4\)，得到：Since \(x=3^a\) and \(a=4\), we get:

\[ x=3^4=81 \]

六、直接验证6. Direct Check

把 \(x=81=3^4\) 代入左边：Substitute \(x=81=3^4\) into the left side:

\[ x^x=81^{81}=(3^4)^{81}=3^{324} \]

右边：The right side:

\[ 3^{x+243}=3^{81+243}=3^{324} \]

左右相等，所以：Both sides are equal, so:

\[ \boxed{x=81} \]

七、最终答案7. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{x=81} \]
