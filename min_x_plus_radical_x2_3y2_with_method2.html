一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：已知 \(x+y=2\)，\(x,y\gt0\)，求 \(x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\) 的最小值。Original image: Given \(x+y=2\), \(x,y\gt0\), find the minimum of \(x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\).
\[ x+y=2,\qquad x,y\gt0 \] \[ \text{求 }x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\text{ 的最小值。} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{3} \]
最小值为 \(3\)，当 \(x=y=1\) 时取得。The minimum is \(3\), achieved when \(x=y=1\).

三、解法一：直接证明不小于 33. Method 1: Prove It Is at Least 3

因为 \(x+y=2\)，所以：Since \(x+y=2\), we have:

\[ y=2-x \]

要证明原式最小为 3，只要证明：To prove the minimum is 3, it is enough to prove:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge 3 \]

也就是：That is:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge 3-x \]

由于 \(0\lt x\lt2\)，所以 \(3-x\gt0\)，可以平方：Since \(0\lt x\lt2\), we have \(3-x\gt0\), so we can square both sides:

\[ x^2+3y^2\ge (3-x)^2 \]

把 \(y=2-x\) 代入并整理：Substitute \(y=2-x\) and simplify:

\[ x^2+3(2-x)^2-(3-x)^2 \]
\[ =x^2+3(x^2-4x+4)-(x^2-6x+9) \]
\[ =3x^2-6x+3 \]
\[ =3(x-1)^2\ge0 \]

所以：Therefore:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge3 \]

当 \(x=1\) 时，由 \(x+y=2\) 得 \(y=1\)，等号成立。When \(x=1\), the condition \(x+y=2\) gives \(y=1\), and equality holds.

因此最小值是 \(3\)。Hence the minimum is \(3\).

四、几何理解4. Geometric Interpretation

根号部分可以看作直角三角形的斜边：两条直角边分别为 \(x\) 和 \(\sqrt3y\)。代数证明中出现的 \(3(x-1)^2\ge0\)，说明最优时 \(x=1\)，从而 \(y=1\)。The radical can be viewed as the hypotenuse of a right triangle with legs \(x\) and \(\sqrt3y\). The algebraic proof produces \(3(x-1)^2\ge0\), showing the optimum occurs at \(x=1\), hence \(y=1\).

五、解法二：按图拼接的几何法5. Method 2: Geometric Construction by Rearranging Segments

补充方法图示：把第一项与根号项分别看成线段，再按 \(30^\circ\) 与直角三角形关系进行拼接。Supplementary diagram: treat the first term and the radical term as segments, then rearrange them using a \(30^\circ\) construction and right-triangle relations.

这个方法的核心思想是：把原式看成两段折线的长度和，再利用“两点之间线段最短”来求最小值。The key idea is to view the expression as the sum of two segments in a broken line, and then use the fact that the straight segment between two fixed points is the shortest path.

\[ E=x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \]

先把第二项 \(\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\) 看成一个直角三角形的斜边，其中两条直角边分别是 \(x\) 和 \(\sqrt3\,y\)。再把第一项 \(x\) 作为另一段长度为 \(x\) 的线段，像图中那样进行旋转与拼接。First, regard \(\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\) as the hypotenuse of a right triangle whose legs are \(x\) and \(\sqrt3\,y\). Then use the first term \(x\) as another segment of length \(x\), and rotate/rearrange the pieces as in the diagram.

在这个构造中，由条件 \(x+y=2\)，拼接后所对应的两个端点之间的直线距离是一个定值；图中它恰好等于 \(3\)。因此，由折线长不小于两端点的直线距离可得：In this construction, the condition \(x+y=2\) makes the distance between the two resulting endpoints a constant; in the diagram, that fixed distance is exactly \(3\). Therefore, since a broken line is at least as long as the straight segment joining its endpoints, we get:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge 3 \]

当图中的两段折线恰好共线时，等号成立，于是原式取得最小值 \(3\)。再结合条件 \(x+y=2\)，可知对应情形为 \(x=1,\ y=1\)。Equality holds when the two segments in the broken line become collinear. Hence the expression reaches its minimum value \(3\). Together with \(x+y=2\), this occurs at \(x=1,\ y=1\).

所以，这个几何拼接法同样得到：最小值是 \(3\)。So this geometric rearrangement method also gives the same result: the minimum is \(3\).

六、导数验证6. Derivative Check

设 \(y=2-x\)，则：Let \(y=2-x\). Then:

\[ f(x)=x+\sqrt{x^2+3(2-x)^2} =x+\sqrt{4x^2-12x+12} \]

求导：Differentiate:

\[ f'(x)=1+\frac{4x-6}{\sqrt{4x^2-12x+12}} \]

令 \(f'(x)=0\)，可得：Set \(f'(x)=0\), giving:

\[ \sqrt{4x^2-12x+12}=6-4x \]

平方整理：After squaring and simplifying:

\[ x^2-3x+2=0 \]
\[ x=1\quad \text{或}\quad x=2 \]

由于 \(0\lt x\lt2\)，内部临界点是 \(x=1\)，此时 \(y=1\)。Since \(0\lt x\lt2\), the interior critical point is \(x=1\), so \(y=1\).

\[ f(1)=1+\sqrt{1+3}=3 \]
