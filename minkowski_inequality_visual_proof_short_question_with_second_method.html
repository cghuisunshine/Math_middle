一、要证明的不等式1. Inequality to Prove

题目短图：证明这个二维形式的闵可夫斯基不等式。Short problem image: prove this 2D form of Minkowski’s inequality.
\[ \sqrt{a^2+b^2}+\sqrt{c^2+d^2} \ge \sqrt{(a+c)^2+(b+d)^2} \]

二、图形解法2. Geometric Method

把两个根号看成两个直角三角形的斜边：View the two radicals as hypotenuses of two right triangles:

\[ \sqrt{a^2+b^2},\qquad \sqrt{c^2+d^2} \]

把它们首尾相接，合成后的水平长度是 \(a+c\)，竖直长度是 \(b+d\)，所以第三边是：Place them head-to-tail. The resulting horizontal length is \(a+c\), and the vertical length is \(b+d\), so the third side is:

\[ \sqrt{(a+c)^2+(b+d)^2} \]
折线长度 ≥ 直线长度，因此不等式成立。A broken line is at least as long as the straight segment, so the inequality holds.

三、方法二：第二张图示解法3. Method 2: The Second Visual Diagram

第二张图把不等式直接看成“三角形两边之和大于等于第三边”。The second diagram views the inequality directly as “the sum of two sides is at least the third side” in a triangle.

第二张图示解法：把两个根式分别作为两条边，合成后的根式作为第三边。Second visual method: the two radicals are two sides, and the combined radical is the third side.

图中三条边分别对应：In the diagram, the three side lengths correspond to:

\[ \text{第一边}=\sqrt{a^2+b^2} \]
\[ \text{第二边}=\sqrt{c^2+d^2} \]
\[ \text{第三边}=\sqrt{(a+c)^2+(b+d)^2} \]

因为从同一起点到同一终点，折线走法不短于直线走法，所以：Since a broken path from one endpoint to another is not shorter than the straight path, we have:

\[ \sqrt{a^2+b^2}+\sqrt{c^2+d^2} \ge \sqrt{(a+c)^2+(b+d)^2} \]
这张图的核心就是：边 + 边 ≥ 边。The core of this diagram is: side + side ≥ side.

等号成立时，两段边刚好排成一条直线，也就是两个直角三角形相似且方向一致。Equality holds when the two segments lie on one straight line, meaning the two right triangles are similar and point in the same direction.

\[ \frac{a}{b}=\frac{c}{d} \]

四、向量法4. Vector Method

令：Let:

\[ \vec u=(a,b),\qquad \vec v=(c,d) \]

则：Then:

\[ |\vec u|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2},\qquad |\vec v|=\sqrt{c^2+d^2} \]
\[ \vec u+\vec v=(a+c,b+d) \]
\[ |\vec u+\vec v|=\sqrt{(a+c)^2+(b+d)^2} \]

由向量三角不等式：By the vector triangle inequality:

\[ |\vec u|+|\vec v|\ge |\vec u+\vec v| \]

代回即可得到原不等式。Substituting back gives the original inequality.

五、代数法：柯西不等式5. Algebraic Method: Cauchy's Inequality

设：Let:

\[ A=\sqrt{a^2+b^2},\qquad B=\sqrt{c^2+d^2} \]

要证明：We want to prove:

\[ A+B\ge \sqrt{(a+c)^2+(b+d)^2} \]

两边非负，平方后只需证明：Both sides are nonnegative. After squaring, it is enough to prove:

\[ AB\ge ac+bd \]

即：That is:

\[ \sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sqrt{c^2+d^2}\ge ac+bd \]

由柯西不等式：By Cauchy's inequality:

\[ (a^2+b^2)(c^2+d^2)\ge (ac+bd)^2 \]

也可以直接看差：We can also check the difference directly:

\[ (a^2+b^2)(c^2+d^2)-(ac+bd)^2 \]
\[ =a^2d^2+b^2c^2-2abcd \]
\[ =(ad-bc)^2\ge0 \]
所以原不等式成立。Therefore, the original inequality holds.

六、等号成立条件6. Equality Condition

等号成立时，两个向量同方向。Equality holds when the two vectors point in the same direction.

\[ (a,b)=k(c,d),\qquad k\ge0 \]

如果 \(a,b,c,d\) 都为正数，也常写成：If \(a,b,c,d\) are all positive, this is often written as:

\[ \frac{a}{b}=\frac{c}{d} \]
图中“\(\frac{a}{b}=\frac{c}{d}\) 时可取等号”，表示两个小直角三角形相似且方向一致。The condition \(\frac{a}{b}=\frac{c}{d}\) means the two right triangles are similar and point in the same direction.

七、最终总结7. Final Summary

\[ \boxed{ \sqrt{a^2+b^2}+\sqrt{c^2+d^2} \ge \sqrt{(a+c)^2+(b+d)^2} } \]
本质：折线长度 ≥ 直线长度。Essence: a broken line is at least as long as the straight line.
