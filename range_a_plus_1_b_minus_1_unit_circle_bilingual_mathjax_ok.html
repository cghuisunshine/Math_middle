已知 \(a^2+b^2=1\)，求 \((a+1)(b-1)\) 的取值范围 Given \(a^2+b^2=1\), Find the Range of \((a+1)(b-1)\)

一、题目1. Problem

题目原图 Original problem image
\[ a,b\in\mathbb{R},\qquad a^2+b^2=1 \]
\[ \text{求 }(a+1)(b-1)\text{ 的取值范围} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{-\frac{3+2\sqrt2}{2}\le (a+1)(b-1)\le 0} \]
取值范围：\(\left[-\dfrac{3+2\sqrt2}{2},\ 0\right]\) Range: \(\left[-\dfrac{3+2\sqrt2}{2},\ 0\right]\)

三、方法一：化成完全平方3. Method 1: Rewrite as a Perfect Square

\[ S=(a+1)(b-1)=ab-a+b-1 \]
\[ u=a+b,\qquad v=a-b \]
\[ u^2+v^2=(a+b)^2+(a-b)^2=2(a^2+b^2)=2 \]
\[ ab=\frac{u^2-v^2}{4},\qquad -a+b=-(a-b)=-v \]
\[ S=\frac{u^2-v^2}{4}-v-1 \]
\[ \text{由 }u^2=2-v^2,\ \ S=\frac{2-v^2-v^2}{4}-v-1 \]
\[ S=\frac{1-v^2}{2}-v-1=-\frac{(v+1)^2}{2} \]
关键结果： Key result: \[ S=-\frac{(v+1)^2}{2} \]
\[ -\sqrt2\le v\le \sqrt2 \]
\[ S_{\max}=0,\qquad S_{\min}=-\frac{(1+\sqrt2)^2}{2}=-\frac{3+2\sqrt2}{2} \]

四、方法二：令 \(x=b-a\)，转化为二次函数4. Method 2: Let \(x=b-a\) and Reduce to a Quadratic Function

令Let

\[ S=(a+1)(b-1) \]

先展开：Expand first:

\[ S=ab-a+b-1 \]

设 \(x=b-a\)，则Let \(x=b-a\). Then

\[ x^2=(b-a)^2=a^2+b^2-2ab=1-2ab \]
\[ 2ab=1-x^2\qquad\Longrightarrow\qquad ab=\frac{1-x^2}{2} \]

代回 \(S\)：Substitute into \(S\):

\[ S=ab+(b-a)-1=\frac{1-x^2}{2}+x-1 \]
\[ S=-\frac12x^2+x-\frac12 \]

下面确定 \(x\) 的范围。由 \((a+b)^2\ge 0\)：Now determine the range of \(x\). Since \((a+b)^2\ge 0\):

\[ a^2+b^2+2ab\ge 0 \]
\[ 1+2ab\ge 0\qquad\Longrightarrow\qquad 2ab\ge -1 \]
\[ x^2=1-2ab\le 1-(-1)=2 \]
\[ x\in[-\sqrt2,\sqrt2] \]

于是只需研究开口向下的二次函数So we only need to study the downward-opening quadratic

\[ S(x)=-\frac12x^2+x-\frac12 \]

对称轴为Its axis is

\[ x=-\frac{1}{2\cdot(-\frac12)}=1 \]

因为 \(1\in[-\sqrt2,\sqrt2]\)，所以最大值在 \(x=1\) 处取得：Since \(1\in[-\sqrt2,\sqrt2]\), the maximum is attained at \(x=1\):

\[ S_{\max}=-\frac12(1)^2+1-\frac12=0 \]

最小值出现在离对称轴最远的端点。比较：The minimum occurs at the endpoint farthest from the axis. Compare:

\[ |-\sqrt2-1|=1+\sqrt2,\qquad |\sqrt2-1|=\sqrt2-1 \]

所以最小值在 \(x=-\sqrt2\) 处取得：So the minimum occurs at \(x=-\sqrt2\):

\[ S_{\min}=-\frac12(-\sqrt2)^2+(-\sqrt2)-\frac12 \]
\[ S_{\min}=-1-\sqrt2-\frac12=-\frac32-\sqrt2=-\frac{3+2\sqrt2}{2} \]
因此： Therefore: \[ \boxed{-\frac{3+2\sqrt2}{2}\le S\le 0} \]

五、最终结论5. Final Conclusion

\[ \boxed{(a+1)(b-1)\in\left[-\frac{3+2\sqrt2}{2},\ 0\right]} \]
