一、题目1. Problem

第一次干了工程的 \(\frac13\)，第二次干了剩下的 \(\frac25\)，剩下的 30 天干完，共需多少天？The first time, \(\frac13\) of the project was completed. The second time, \(\frac25\) of the remaining project was completed. The rest took 30 days. How many days are needed in total?
\[ \text{第一次干了工程的 }\frac13；\quad \text{第二次干了剩下的 }\frac25；\quad \text{剩下的 30 天干完。求共需多少天？} \]

二、解法一：求最后剩下的是全工程的几分之几2. Solution 1: Find the Final Remaining Fraction

把整个工程看作单位“1”。第一次干了 \(\frac13\)，还剩：Treat the whole project as 1. After the first time, the remaining part is:

\[1-\frac13=\frac23\]

第二次干了“剩下的” \(\frac25\)，所以第二次后还剩下“剩下部分”的：The second time completed \(\frac25\) of the remaining part, so after that, the remaining part is:

\[1-\frac25=\frac35\]

因此最后剩下的是全工程的：Therefore, the final remaining part of the whole project is:

\[\frac23\times\frac35=\frac25\]

这 \(\frac25\) 的工程需要 30 天完成，所以全工程需要：This \(\frac25\) of the project takes 30 days, so the whole project takes:

\[30\div\frac25=30\times\frac52=75\]
答案：75 天Answer: 75 days

四、解法二：倒推法4. Solution 2: Work Backward

第二次后剩下的是第一次后剩下部分的 \(\frac35\)，它对应 30 天，所以第一次之后剩下的部分对应：After the second time, \(\frac35\) of the previous remaining part is left, and it corresponds to 30 days. So the part left after the first time corresponds to:

\[30\div\frac35=50\text{ 天}\]

第一次之后还剩全工程的 \(\frac23\)，这 \(\frac23\) 对应 50 天，所以全工程对应：After the first time, \(\frac23\) of the whole project remained. This \(\frac23\) corresponds to 50 days, so the whole project corresponds to:

\[50\div\frac23=75\text{ 天}\]
答案：75 天Answer: 75 days

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

\[\boxed{75\text{ 天}}\]
最终答案：75 天Final answer: 75 days
