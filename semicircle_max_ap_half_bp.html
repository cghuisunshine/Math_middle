一、题目1. Problem

半圆半径为 1，求 \(AP+\frac12BP\) 的最大值。The radius of the semicircle is 1. Find the maximum of \(AP+\frac12BP\).
\[ \text{半圆半径为 }1，\quad \text{求 }AP+\frac12BP\text{ 的最大值。} \]

二、关键观察2. Key Observation

因为半径为 1，所以直径 \(AB=2\)。点 \(P\) 在半圆上，因此 \(\angle APB=90^\circ\)。The radius is 1, so the diameter \(AB=2\). Since \(P\) is on the semicircle, \(\angle APB=90^\circ\).

\[ AP^2+BP^2=AB^2=4 \]

三、解法一：设两边长度，用柯西不等式3. Solution 1: Cauchy's Inequality

设 \(AP=x, BP=y\)，则：Let \(AP=x, BP=y\). Then:

\[ x^2+y^2=4 \]

要求最大值：We want to maximize:

\[ x+\frac12y \]

由柯西不等式：By Cauchy's inequality:

\[ x+\frac12y \le \sqrt{x^2+y^2}\sqrt{1^2+\left(\frac12\right)^2} = 2\cdot\frac{\sqrt5}2 = \sqrt5 \]

当 \(x:y=1:\frac12=2:1\) 时取等号。Equality holds when \(x:y=1:\frac12=2:1\).

最大值：\(\sqrt5\)Maximum value: \(\sqrt5\)

四、解法二：三角函数4. Solution 2: Trigonometry

设 \(\angle PAB=u\)。在直角三角形 \(APB\) 中，斜边 \(AB=2\)，所以：Let \(\angle PAB=u\). In right triangle \(APB\), the hypotenuse is \(AB=2\), so:

\[ AP=2\cos u,\qquad BP=2\sin u \]
\[ AP+\frac12BP=2\cos u+\sin u \]
\[ 2\cos u+\sin u \le \sqrt{2^2+1^2}\sqrt{\cos^2u+\sin^2u} =\sqrt5 \]

当 \(\tan u=\frac12\) 时取等号。Equality holds when \(\tan u=\frac12\).

最大值：\(\sqrt5\)Maximum value: \(\sqrt5\)

五、解法三：只用 \(a^2+b^2\ge 2ab\)5. Solution 3: Using Only \(a^2+b^2\ge 2ab\)

设：Let:

\[ AP=x,\qquad BP=y \]

因为 \(\angle APB=90^\circ\)，且 \(AB=2\)，所以：Since \(\angle APB=90^\circ\) and \(AB=2\):

\[ x^2+y^2=4 \]

要最大化：We want to maximize:

\[ S=x+\frac12y \]

先平方：Square it first:

\[ S^2=\left(x+\frac12y\right)^2 =x^2+xy+\frac14y^2 \]

只用基本不等式 \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\)。取 \(a=\frac{x}{2}\)，\(b=y\)，得到：Use only \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\). Take \(a=\frac{x}{2}\), \(b=y\), then:

\[ \left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^2+y^2 \ge 2\cdot\frac{x}{2}\cdot y \]
\[ \frac14x^2+y^2\ge xy \]

所以：So:

\[ S^2 =x^2+xy+\frac14y^2 \le x^2+\left(\frac14x^2+y^2\right)+\frac14y^2 \]
\[ S^2\le \frac54x^2+\frac54y^2 = \frac54(x^2+y^2) = \frac54\cdot4 =5 \]

因此：Therefore:

\[ S\le \sqrt5 \]

当 \(\frac{x}{2}=y\)，也就是 \(x:y=2:1\) 时取等号。Equality holds when \(\frac{x}{2}=y\), i.e. \(x:y=2:1\).

最大值：\(\sqrt5\)Maximum value: \(\sqrt5\)
