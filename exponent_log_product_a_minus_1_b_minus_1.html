一、题目1. Problem

若 5ᵃ=35，7ᵇ=35，求 (a−1)(b−1)。 If 5ᵃ=35 and 7ᵇ=35, find (a−1)(b−1).
若 5ᵃ = 35，7ᵇ = 35 求：(a - 1)(b - 1)

二、解法一：直接转化为对数2. Solution 1: Convert to Logarithms

因为 35=5×7，所以：Since 35=5×7:

5ᵃ = 35 = 5×7

两边取以 5 为底的对数：Take logarithm base 5 on both sides:

a = log₅35 = log₅(5×7) = log₅5 + log₅7 = 1 + log₅7

所以：Therefore:

a - 1 = log₅7

同理，因为 35=7×5：Similarly, since 35=7×5:

b = log₇35 = log₇(7×5) = 1 + log₇5

所以：Therefore:

b - 1 = log₇5

于是：Thus:

(a-1)(b-1) = log₅7 · log₇5

三、关键性质：互为倒数3. Key Property: Reciprocals

对数有一个常用性质：A useful logarithm property is:

logₐb · logᵦa = 1

因为：Because:

log₇5 = 1 / log₅7

所以：So:

log₅7 · log₇5 = log₅7 · 1/log₅7 = 1

四、解法二：不写 log，用指数对应4. Solution 2: Use Exponent Correspondence

由 5ᵃ=35=5×7：From 5ᵃ=35=5×7:

5ᵃ = 5×7 5ᵃ / 5 = 7 5ᵃ⁻¹ = 7

由 7ᵇ=35=7×5：From 7ᵇ=35=7×5:

7ᵇ = 7×5 7ᵇ / 7 = 5 7ᵇ⁻¹ = 5

因此：Therefore:

5ᵃ⁻¹ = 7 7ᵇ⁻¹ = 5

把第二个式子的 7 换成 5ᵃ⁻¹：Replace 7 in the second equation by 5ᵃ⁻¹:

7ᵇ⁻¹ = 5 (5ᵃ⁻¹)ᵇ⁻¹ = 5 5^(a-1)(b-1) = 5¹

因为底数相同，所以指数相等：Since the bases are the same, the exponents are equal:

(a-1)(b-1) = 1

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

(a - 1)(b - 1) = 1
