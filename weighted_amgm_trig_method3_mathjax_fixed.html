一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[ x\gt0,\quad y\gt0,\quad x^2+y^2=1 \]
\[ \text{求 }(x+1)(5y+2)\text{ 的最大值} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac{48}{5}} \]
当 \(x=\frac35,\ y=\frac45\) 时取得最大值。 The maximum is attained at \(x=\frac35,\ y=\frac45\).

三、配权 AM-GM 的基本想法3. The Basic Idea of Weighted AM-GM

先展开原式：

First expand the expression:

\[ (x+1)(5y+2)=5xy+2x+5y+2 \]

我们使用最基本的不等式：

We use the basic inequality:

\[ 2uv\le u^2+v^2 \]

“配权”的意思是：把每一项改写成合适的两个量的乘积，使它们在同一个点同时取等号，并使最后的 \(x^2,y^2\) 系数相同。

“Weighted” means rewriting each term as a product of two suitable quantities so that all inequalities become equalities at the same point and the final coefficients of \(x^2\) and \(y^2\) match.

本题希望最终凑成 \(5(x^2+y^2)\)，这样就能直接使用 \(x^2+y^2=1\)。 Here we want to produce \(5(x^2+y^2)\), so that the condition \(x^2+y^2=1\) can be used immediately.

四、配权 AM-GM 的完整解法4. Complete Weighted AM-GM Solution

配权 AM-GM 手写解法Handwritten weighted AM-GM solution

先展开：

First expand:

\[ (x+1)(5y+2)=5xy+2x+5y+2 \]

为了使三次使用 \(2uv\le u^2+v^2\) 时能同时取等，我们选择使等号条件为：

To make all three uses of \(2uv\le u^2+v^2\) attain equality simultaneously, we choose the equality conditions:

\[ 4x=3y,\qquad x=\frac35,\qquad y=\frac45 \]

这三个条件互相一致，而且满足 \(x^2+y^2=1\)。于是把每一项按这些等号条件进行配权。

These conditions are consistent with one another and satisfy \(x^2+y^2=1\). We therefore weight each term according to these equality conditions.

\[ 5xy = \frac5{12}(4x)(3y) \le \frac5{24}(16x^2+9y^2) \]
\[ 2x = \frac{10}{3}x\cdot\frac35 \le \frac{10}{6}\left(x^2+\frac9{25}\right) \]
\[ 5y = \frac{25}{4}\cdot\frac45\,y \le \frac{25}{8}\left(y^2+\frac{16}{25}\right) \]

三式相加：

Add the three inequalities:

\[ (x+1)(5y+2) \le \frac5{24}(16x^2+9y^2) + \frac{10}{6}\left(x^2+\frac9{25}\right) + \frac{25}{8}\left(y^2+\frac{16}{25}\right) +2 \]

整理 \(x^2,y^2\) 系数：

Collect the coefficients of \(x^2,y^2\):

\[ =5x^2+5y^2+\frac35+2+2 \]
\[ =5(x^2+y^2)+\frac{23}{5} \]
\[ =5+\frac{23}{5} =\frac{48}{5} \]
三个不等式在 \(x=\frac35,\ y=\frac45\) 时同时取等号，所以最大值确实是 \(\boxed{\frac{48}{5}}\)。 All three inequalities become equalities at \(x=\frac35,\ y=\frac45\), so the true maximum is \(\boxed{\frac{48}{5}}\).

五、这些权重是怎样得到的？5. How Are These Weights Chosen?

对于 \(5xy\)，希望 AM-GM 的等号条件给出 \(y:x=4:3\)。因此把两个量选成 \(4x\) 和 \(3y\)：

For \(5xy\), we want the AM-GM equality condition to give \(y:x=4:3\). So choose the two quantities \(4x\) and \(3y\):

\[ 2(4x)(3y)\le (4x)^2+(3y)^2 \]

因为 \(2(4x)(3y)=24xy\)，所以乘以 \(\frac5{24}\)：

Since \(2(4x)(3y)=24xy\), multiply by \(\frac5{24}\):

\[ 5xy\le \frac5{24}(16x^2+9y^2) \]

对于 \(2x\)，希望在 \(x=\frac35\) 时取等号，所以使用 \(x\) 和 \(\frac35\)：

For \(2x\), we want equality at \(x=\frac35\), so use \(x\) and \(\frac35\):

\[ 2x\cdot\frac35\le x^2+\frac9{25} \]

左边是 \(\frac65x\)。为了变成 \(2x\)，乘以 \(\frac53\)：

The left side is \(\frac65x\). To make it \(2x\), multiply by \(\frac53\):

\[ 2x\le \frac53\left(x^2+\frac9{25}\right) \]

对于 \(5y\)，希望在 \(y=\frac45\) 时取等号，所以使用 \(y\) 和 \(\frac45\)：

For \(5y\), we want equality at \(y=\frac45\), so use \(y\) and \(\frac45\):

\[ 2y\cdot\frac45\le y^2+\frac{16}{25} \]

左边是 \(\frac85y\)。为了变成 \(5y\)，乘以 \(\frac{25}{8}\)：

The left side is \(\frac85y\). To make it \(5y\), multiply by \(\frac{25}{8}\):

\[ 5y\le \frac{25}{8}\left(y^2+\frac{16}{25}\right) \]
所以配权的来源是：先规定每一步的等号条件，再把乘积前面的系数调回原来的 \(5xy,2x,5y\)。 Thus the weights come from first fixing the equality condition for each step, then adjusting the outside coefficient so the left side returns to \(5xy,2x,5y\).

六、一般怎样求比例参数 \(s\)？6. General Method for Finding the Ratio Parameter \(s\)

设最优点满足：

Assume the optimal point satisfies:

\[ y=sx,\qquad s\gt0 \]

由 \(x^2+y^2=1\)：

From \(x^2+y^2=1\):

\[ x_0=\frac1{\sqrt{1+s^2}},\qquad y_0=\frac{s}{\sqrt{1+s^2}} \]

接下来把 \(5xy,2x,5y\) 都配成在 \((x_0,y_0)\) 处取等号的 AM-GM 形式。

Now weight \(5xy,2x,5y\) so that each AM-GM inequality attains equality at \((x_0,y_0)\).

1. 对 \(5xy\) 配权

1. Weight the term \(5xy\)

为了让等号条件是 \(y=sx\)，使用：

To make the equality condition \(y=sx\), use:

\[ 2xy\le sx^2+\frac1s y^2 \]
\[ 5xy\le \frac{5s}{2}x^2+ \frac{5}{2s}y^2 \]

2. 对 \(2x\) 配权

2. Weight the term \(2x\)

为了在 \(x=x_0\) 时取等号：

To attain equality when \(x=x_0\):

\[ 2xx_0\le x^2+x_0^2 \]

两边除以 \(x_0\)：

Divide by \(x_0\):

\[ 2x\le \frac1{x_0}x^2+x_0 = \sqrt{1+s^2}\,x^2+ \frac1{\sqrt{1+s^2}} \]

3. 对 \(5y\) 配权

3. Weight the term \(5y\)

为了在 \(y=y_0\) 时取等号：

To attain equality when \(y=y_0\):

\[ 2yy_0\le y^2+y_0^2 \]

乘以 \(\frac5{2y_0}\)：

Multiply by \(\frac5{2y_0}\):

\[ 5y\le \frac5{2y_0}y^2+\frac{5y_0}{2} = \frac{5\sqrt{1+s^2}}{2s}y^2+ \frac{5s}{2\sqrt{1+s^2}} \]

4. 令 \(x^2,y^2\) 的总系数相等

4. Make the total coefficients of \(x^2,y^2\) equal

这样才能合并成 \(C(x^2+y^2)\)。

This allows the result to combine into \(C(x^2+y^2)\).

\(x^2\) 的总系数是：

The total coefficient of \(x^2\) is:

\[ A(s)=\frac{5s}{2}+\sqrt{1+s^2} \]

\(y^2\) 的总系数是：

The total coefficient of \(y^2\) is:

\[ B(s)= \frac5{2s}+ \frac{5\sqrt{1+s^2}}{2s} \]

令 \(A(s)=B(s)\)：

Set \(A(s)=B(s)\):

\[ \frac{5s}{2}+\sqrt{1+s^2} = \frac5{2s}+ \frac{5\sqrt{1+s^2}}{2s} \]

两边乘以 \(2s\)：

Multiply both sides by \(2s\):

\[ 5s^2+2s\sqrt{1+s^2} = 5+5\sqrt{1+s^2} \]
\[ 5(s^2-1) = (5-2s)\sqrt{1+s^2} \]

解得正数：

The positive solution is:

\[ s=\frac43 \]

所以：

Therefore:

\[ y=\frac43x \]
\[ x=\frac35,\qquad y=\frac45 \]
一般步骤：设 \(y=sx\) → 写出取等点 → 为每一项配权 → 令 \(x^2,y^2\) 的总系数相等 → 解出 \(s\)。 General procedure: set \(y=sx\) → write the equality point → weight each term → equate the total coefficients of \(x^2,y^2\) → solve for \(s\).

七、方法三：三角代换 + 万能代换 + 导数7. Method 3: Trigonometric and Weierstrass Substitution with Calculus

你提供的三角代换解法 Your trigonometric-substitution solution

由 \(x^2+y^2=1,\ x\gt0,\ y\gt0\)，设：

Since \(x^2+y^2=1,\ x\gt0,\ y\gt0\), let:

$$ x=\cos\theta,\qquad y=\sin\theta,\qquad 0\lt\theta\lt\frac{\pi}{2} $$

再令：

Now let:

$$ t=\tan\frac{\theta}{2}, \qquad 0\lt t\lt 1 $$

利用万能代换公式：

Using the Weierstrass substitution formulas:

$$ \cos\theta=\frac{1-t^2}{1+t^2}, \qquad \sin\theta=\frac{2t}{1+t^2} $$

原式变为：

The expression becomes:

$$ (x+1)(5y+2) = \left(\frac{1-t^2}{1+t^2}+1\right) \left(5\cdot\frac{2t}{1+t^2}+2\right) $$
$$ = \frac{2}{1+t^2} \cdot \frac{10t+2+2t^2}{1+t^2} $$
$$ = \frac{4(t^2+5t+1)}{(1+t^2)^2} =f(t) $$

求导：

Differentiate:

$$ f'(t) = -\frac{4(2t-1)(t^2+8t+5)}{(1+t^2)^3} $$

在 \(0\lt t\lt1\) 内，\(t^2+8t+5\gt0\)，且 \((1+t^2)^3\gt0\)，所以 \(f'(t)\) 的符号只由 \(2t-1\) 决定。

For \(0\lt t\lt1\), both \(t^2+8t+5\gt0\) and \((1+t^2)^3\gt0\), so the sign of \(f'(t)\) is determined by \(2t-1\).

$$ f'(t)\gt0\quad\left(0\lt t\lt\frac12\right) $$
$$ f'(t)=0\quad\left(t=\frac12\right) $$
$$ f'(t)\lt0\quad\left(\frac12\lt t\lt1\right) $$

所以 \(f(t)\) 先增后减，在 \(t=\frac12\) 时取得最大值。

Thus \(f(t)\) first increases and then decreases, so its maximum occurs at \(t=\frac12\).

$$ f\left(\frac12\right) = \frac{4\left(\frac14+\frac52+1\right)} {\left(1+\frac14\right)^2} = \frac{48}{5} $$

此时：

At this point:

$$ x= \frac{1-\frac14}{1+\frac14} = \frac35 $$
$$ y= \frac{1}{1+\frac14} = \frac45 $$
所以最大值为 \(\boxed{\frac{48}{5}}\)，在 \(x=\frac35,\ y=\frac45\) 时取得。 Therefore, the maximum is \(\boxed{\frac{48}{5}}\), attained at \(x=\frac35,\ y=\frac45\).

\[ \boxed{\displaystyle \max_{x^2+y^2=1}(x+1)(5y+2)=\frac{48}{5}} \]
