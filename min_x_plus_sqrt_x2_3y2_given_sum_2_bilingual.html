一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[x+y=2,\qquad x>0,\ y>0\]
\[\text{求 }x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\text{ 的最小值}\]

二、答案2. Answer

\[\boxed{3}\]
最小值为 \(3\)，当 \(x=1,\ y=1\) 时取得。The minimum value is \(3\), achieved when \(x=1,\ y=1\).

三、先猜最小值3. First Guess the Minimum

因为 \(x+y=2\)，最对称的取值是：Since \(x+y=2\), the most symmetric choice is:

\[x=1,\qquad y=1\]

代入：Substitute:

\[x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}=1+\sqrt{1+3}=3\]
所以猜最小值是 \(3\)。接下来证明它永远不小于 \(3\)。So we guess the minimum is \(3\). Now we prove the expression is always at least \(3\).

四、关键证明4. Key Proof

要证：We want to prove:

\[x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge 3\]

等价于证：This is equivalent to proving:

\[\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge 3-x\]

因为 \(x+y=2\) 且 \(x,y>0\)，所以 \(00\)，可以平方比较。Since \(x+y=2\) and \(x,y>0\), we have \(00\). We can compare by squaring.

五、平方比较5. Compare by Squaring

由 \(y=2-x\)，比较：Using \(y=2-x\), compare:

\[x^2+3y^2-(3-x)^2\]
\[=x^2+3(2-x)^2-(3-x)^2\]
\[=3x^2-6x+3\]
\[=3(x-1)^2\ge0\]

所以：Therefore:

\[x^2+3y^2\ge(3-x)^2\]
\[\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge3-x\]

六、得到下界6. Get the Lower Bound

\[x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge x+(3-x)=3\]
所以目标式一定不小于 \(3\)。So the target expression is always at least \(3\).

七、等号成立7. Equality Case

等号来自：Equality comes from:

\[3(x-1)^2=0\]
\[x=1\]

又由 \(x+y=2\)，得：Since \(x+y=2\), we get:

\[y=1\]
\[1+\sqrt{1+3}=3\]

八、另一种写法：用 \(y\) 表示8. Another Version: Use \(y\)

因为 \(x=2-y\)，也可以证明：Since \(x=2-y\), we can also prove:

\[\sqrt{(2-y)^2+3y^2}\ge 1+y\]

平方比较：Compare by squaring:

\[(2-y)^2+3y^2-(1+y)^2=3(y-1)^2\ge0\]
这和前面的做法完全一样，只是换成了 \(y\) 来写。This is exactly the same idea, written in terms of \(y\).

九、方法二：柯西不等式9. Method 2: Cauchy Inequality

这种方法更快。把根号里的式子看成四个平方的和： This method is faster. View the expression under the radical as a sum of four squares:

\[ x^2+3y^2=x^2+y^2+y^2+y^2 \]

由柯西不等式： By Cauchy's inequality:

\[ (x+y+y+y)^2 \le (1^2+1^2+1^2+1^2)(x^2+y^2+y^2+y^2) \]
\[ (x+3y)^2\le 4(x^2+3y^2) \]

因为 \(x,y>0\)，所以 \(x+3y>0\)，两边开方： Since \(x,y>0\), we have \(x+3y>0\). Taking square roots:

\[ x+3y\le 2\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \]
\[ \sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge \frac{x+3y}{2} \]

因此原式： Therefore, the original expression satisfies:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \ge x+\frac{x+3y}{2} \]
\[ = \frac{3x+3y}{2} = \frac32(x+y) \]

又因为 \(x+y=2\)，所以： Since \(x+y=2\), we get:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \ge \frac32\cdot2=3 \]
当 \(x=y=1\) 时，柯西不等式取等号，所以最小值为 \(3\)。 When \(x=y=1\), equality holds in Cauchy's inequality, so the minimum is \(3\).
这个解法的关键是把 \(3y^2\) 拆成 \(y^2+y^2+y^2\)，这样可以和 \(x+y+y+y=x+3y\) 配合使用柯西不等式。 The key is to split \(3y^2\) as \(y^2+y^2+y^2\), so it pairs with \(x+y+y+y=x+3y\) in Cauchy's inequality.

十、方法三：几何折线法10. Method 3: Geometric Broken-Line Method

图一：把 \(x\) 和 \(\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\) 都表示成线段。 Figure 1: Represent \(x\) and \(\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\) as line segments.
图二：把折线拉直，用点到直线的最短距离求下界。 Figure 2: Straighten the broken line and use the shortest distance to a line.

构造一个右三角形，使它的竖直边为 \(x\)，水平边为 \(\sqrt3x\)。因为 Construct a right triangle whose vertical side is \(x\) and horizontal side is \(\sqrt3x\). Since

\[ \tan 30^\circ=\frac{x}{\sqrt3x}=\frac1{\sqrt3}, \]

所以左边的斜边与水平线夹角为 \(30^\circ\)。 the left slanted side makes a \(30^\circ\) angle with the horizontal line.

再把右边水平边取为 \(\sqrt3y\)，于是右边斜线的长度为： Now take the right horizontal side to be \(\sqrt3y\). Then the right slanted segment has length:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+(\sqrt3y)^2}=\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}. \]

因此目标式 Therefore the target expression

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \]

可以看成一条折线的长度：先走一段长度为 \(x\)，再走一段长度为 \(\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\)。 can be seen as the length of a broken line: first a segment of length \(x\), then a segment of length \(\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\).

关键构造：过左端点作一条与水平线成 \(60^\circ\) 的虚线。图中从上方点到这条虚线的垂线长度正好是 \(x\)。 Key construction: draw a dashed line through the left endpoint making a \(60^\circ\) angle with the horizontal line. The perpendicular distance from the upper point to this dashed line is exactly \(x\).

于是原来的两段长度可以看成： So the two lengths can be interpreted as:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}=QP+PR, \]

其中 \(Q\) 在 \(60^\circ\) 虚线上，\(QP\perp\) 虚线，且 \(QP=x\)。由三角形两边之和不小于第三边： where \(Q\) lies on the \(60^\circ\) dashed line, \(QP\) is perpendicular to that line, and \(QP=x\). By the triangle inequality:

\[ QP+PR\ge QR. \]

而从右端点 \(R\) 到这条 \(60^\circ\) 虚线的最短距离，就是垂线段。由于 The shortest distance from the right endpoint \(R\) to the \(60^\circ\) dashed line is the perpendicular distance. Since

\[ \text{水平总长}=\sqrt3x+\sqrt3y=\sqrt3(x+y)=2\sqrt3, \]

所以点 \(R\) 到 \(60^\circ\) 直线的距离为： the distance from \(R\) to the \(60^\circ\) line is:

\[ 2\sqrt3\cdot\sin60^\circ=2\sqrt3\cdot\frac{\sqrt3}{2}=3. \]

因此： Therefore:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge3. \]
当折线变成这条垂线时取等号，此时 \(x=y=1\)，所以最小值为 \(3\)。 Equality holds when the broken line becomes this perpendicular segment. Then \(x=y=1\), so the minimum is \(3\).

十一、最终答案11. Final Answer

\[\boxed{3}\]
最小值为 \(3\)，当 \(x=1,\ y=1\) 时取得。The minimum value is \(3\), achieved when \(x=1,\ y=1\).
