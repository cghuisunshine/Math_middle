一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[ x+y=2,\qquad x>0,\ y>0 \]
\[ \text{求 }x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\text{ 的最小值} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{3} \]
最小值为 \(3\)，当 \(x=y=1\) 时取得。The minimum value is \(3\), attained at \(x=y=1\).

三、方法一：柯西不等式（已知要凑出 3）3. Method 1: Cauchy Inequality

因为 \(x+y=2\)，所以 \(3=\frac32(x+y)\)。只需证明：Since \(x+y=2\), we have \(3=\frac32(x+y)\). It suffices to prove:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge \frac32(x+y) \]

移项得到：Rearranging gives:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge \frac12x+\frac32y \]
\[ \sqrt{x^2+3y^2}=\sqrt{x^2+(\sqrt3y)^2} \]

由柯西不等式：By Cauchy's inequality:

\[ \frac12x+\frac{\sqrt3}{2}(\sqrt3y) \le \sqrt{\left(\frac12\right)^2+\left(\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\right)^2}\sqrt{x^2+(\sqrt3y)^2} \]
\[ \frac12x+\frac32y\le\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \]

因此：Therefore:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge x+\frac12x+\frac32y=\frac32(x+y)=3 \]

四、方法二：先设最小值为 \(K\)，再反推柯西系数4. Method 2: Let the Minimum Be \(K\), Then Reverse-Engineer Cauchy

诀窍：不要先猜 \(3\)，而是先设最小值为 \(K\)，再倒推柯西不等式里的系数。Key idea: do not guess \(3\) first. Let the minimum be \(K\), then work backward to find the Cauchy coefficients.

因为 \(x+y=2\)，若要证明原式 \(\ge K\)，等价于证明：Since \(x+y=2\), proving the expression is at least \(K\) is equivalent to proving:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge \frac K2(x+y) \]

移项：Rearrange:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge \left(\frac K2-1\right)x+\frac K2y \]

写成向量长度：Write the radical as a vector norm:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+3y^2}=\sqrt{x^2+(\sqrt3y)^2} \]

若用柯西构造Using Cauchy, construct

\[ ux+v(\sqrt3y)\le \sqrt{x^2+(\sqrt3y)^2},\qquad u^2+v^2=1 \]

为了刚好得到右边，需要：To match the desired right-hand side, we need:

\[ u=\frac K2-1,\qquad v=\frac{K}{2\sqrt3} \]

代入 \(u^2+v^2=1\)：Substitute into \(u^2+v^2=1\):

\[ \left(\frac K2-1\right)^2+\left(\frac{K}{2\sqrt3}\right)^2=1 \]
\[ \frac{K^2}{4}-K+1+\frac{K^2}{12}=1 \]
\[ \frac{K^2}{3}-K=0 \quad\Longrightarrow\quad K\left(\frac K3-1\right)=0 \]

因为最小值不是 \(0\)，所以：Since the minimum is not \(0\), we get:

\[ K=3 \]
\[ u=\frac12,\qquad v=\frac{\sqrt3}{2} \]

这就反推出了方法一中的柯西构造。This reverse-engineers exactly the Cauchy construction in Method 1.

五、方法三：设 \(t\) 后用判别式5. Method 3: Introduce \(t\) and Use the Discriminant

手写思路：设 \(t=x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\)Handwritten idea: let \(t=x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\)

设Let

\[ t=x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \]

由 \(x+y=2\)，得 \(y=2-x\)。于是：From \(x+y=2\), \(y=2-x\). Hence:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+3(2-x)^2}=t-x \]

两边平方：Square both sides:

\[ x^2+3(2-x)^2=(t-x)^2 \]
\[ 3x^2+(2t-12)x+12-t^2=0 \]

这是关于 \(x\) 的一元二次方程。要有实数解，必须 \(\Delta\ge0\)：This is a quadratic in \(x\). For a real solution, \(\Delta\ge0\):

\[ \Delta=(2t-12)^2-12(12-t^2)\ge0 \]
\[ 16t^2-48t\ge0 \]
\[ 16t(t-3)\ge0 \]

又因为 \(t>0\)，所以 \(t\ge3\)。Since \(t>0\), we get \(t\ge3\).

六、方法四：直接放缩到 \(\frac{x+3y}{2}\)6. Method 4: Estimate the Radical by \(\frac{x+3y}{2}\)

手写思路：把根号部分放缩为 \(\frac{x+3y}{2}\)（方向已修正）Handwritten idea: bound the radical by \(\frac{x+3y}{2}\) (orientation fixed)

证明：Prove:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge \frac{x+3y}{2} \]

比较平方：Compare squares:

\[ x^2+3y^2-\left(\frac{x+3y}{2}\right)^2 = \frac{4x^2+12y^2-(x+3y)^2}{4} \]
\[ = \frac{3x^2-6xy+3y^2}{4} = \frac{3(x-y)^2}{4}\ge0 \]

因此：Therefore:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \ge x+\frac{x+3y}{2} = \frac32(x+y)=3 \]

七、方法五：几何法7. Method 5: Geometry

手写思路：用几何构造和三角形两边之和大于第三边Handwritten idea: use a geometric construction and the triangle inequality

按图作几何构造，可把原式看成两条线段之和：Following the construction, the expression becomes the sum of two segments:

\[ AD=x,\qquad AC=\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \]
\[ AD+AC=x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2} \]

图中底边：In the figure, the base is:

\[ BC=\sqrt3x+\sqrt3y=\sqrt3(x+y)=2\sqrt3 \]

且 \(\angle EBC=60^\circ\)，所以：Since \(\angle EBC=60^\circ\), we have:

\[ CE=BC\sin60^\circ=2\sqrt3\cdot\frac{\sqrt3}{2}=3 \]

由三角形两边之和大于等于第三边：By the triangle inequality:

\[ AD+AC\ge CE \]
\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}\ge3 \]

八、等号成立条件8. Equality Condition

等号成立时 \(x=y\)。结合 \(x+y=2\)：Equality occurs when \(x=y\). Together with \(x+y=2\):

\[ x=y=1 \]

此时：Then:

\[ x+\sqrt{x^2+3y^2}=1+\sqrt{1+3}=3 \]

九、最终答案9. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{3} \]
最终答案：\(\boxed{3}\)，当 \(x=y=1\) 时取得。Final answer: \(\boxed{3}\), attained when \(x=y=1\).
