一、题目1. Problem

题目原图Original problem image
\[ a^2+b^2=c^2,\qquad a,b,c>0 \]
\[ \text{求 }\frac{c}{a+b}\text{ 的最小值} \]

二、答案2. Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac{\sqrt2}{2}} \]
最小值为 \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\)，当 \(a=b\) 时取得。The minimum is \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\), achieved when \(a=b\).

三、方法一：柯西不等式3. Method 1: Cauchy Inequality

要让 \(\frac{c}{a+b}\) 尽量小，就要让分母 \(a+b\) 相对于 \(c\) 尽量大。To make \(\frac{c}{a+b}\) as small as possible, we want \(a+b\) to be as large as possible relative to \(c\).

由柯西不等式：By Cauchy's inequality:

\[ (a+b)^2\le (1^2+1^2)(a^2+b^2) \]
\[ (a+b)^2\le 2(a^2+b^2) \]

又因为 \(a^2+b^2=c^2\)，所以：Since \(a^2+b^2=c^2\), we get:

\[ (a+b)^2\le 2c^2 \]
\[ a+b\le \sqrt2\,c \]

于是：Therefore:

\[ \frac{c}{a+b}\ge \frac{c}{\sqrt2c}=\frac1{\sqrt2}=\frac{\sqrt2}{2} \]
所以最小值至少是 \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\)。So the minimum is at least \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\).

四、等号成立条件4. Equality Condition

柯西不等式取等号时，需要：Equality in Cauchy's inequality requires:

\[ \frac a1=\frac b1 \]
\[ a=b \]

当 \(a=b\) 时，设 \(a=b=t>0\)，则：When \(a=b\), let \(a=b=t>0\). Then:

\[ c=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}=\sqrt{2t^2}=t\sqrt2 \]
\[ \frac c{a+b}=\frac{t\sqrt2}{2t}=\frac{\sqrt2}{2} \]
因此 \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\) 可以取到，确实是最小值。Therefore \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\) is attainable, so it is truly the minimum.

五、方法二：几何理解5. Method 2: Geometric Understanding

因为 \(a^2+b^2=c^2\)，可以把 \(a,b,c\) 看成一个直角三角形的两条直角边和斜边。Since \(a^2+b^2=c^2\), we can view \(a,b,c\) as the legs and hypotenuse of a right triangle.

对于固定的斜边 \(c\)，要让 \(\frac c{a+b}\) 最小，就要让 \(a+b\) 最大。两条直角边越接近，和越大；最大时 \(a=b\)。For a fixed hypotenuse \(c\), minimizing \(\frac c{a+b}\) means maximizing \(a+b\). The two legs have the largest sum when they are equal, so \(a=b\).

\[ a=b\Rightarrow c=\sqrt2a \]
\[ \frac c{a+b}=\frac{\sqrt2a}{2a}=\frac{\sqrt2}{2} \]

六、方法三：平方展开法6. Method 3: Expanding \((a+b)^2\)

这个方法不用直接引用柯西，而是从平方展开出发。 This method does not directly quote Cauchy's inequality; it starts from expanding a square.

\[ (a+b)^2=a^2+b^2+2ab \]

因为 \(a,b>0\)，且 Since \(a,b>0\), and

\[ a^2+b^2\ge 2ab \]

所以： therefore:

\[ (a+b)^2=a^2+b^2+2ab \le a^2+b^2+(a^2+b^2) \]
\[ (a+b)^2\le 2(a^2+b^2) \]

又因为 \(a^2+b^2=c^2\)，所以： Since \(a^2+b^2=c^2\), we get:

\[ (a+b)^2\le 2c^2 \]

两边取倒数时，不等号方向会改变： When taking reciprocals of positive quantities, the inequality direction reverses:

\[ \frac{1}{(a+b)^2}\ge \frac{1}{2c^2} \]

两边乘以 \(c^2\)： Multiply both sides by \(c^2\):

\[ \frac{c^2}{(a+b)^2}\ge \frac12 \]

因为 \(\frac{c}{a+b}>0\)，所以开平方： Since \(\frac{c}{a+b}>0\), take square roots:

\[ \frac{c}{a+b}\ge \frac1{\sqrt2}=\frac{\sqrt2}{2} \]
当 \(a=b\) 时，\(a^2+b^2=2ab\) 取等号，所以最小值仍为 \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\)。 When \(a=b\), equality holds in \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\), so the minimum is still \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\).
这个方法其实是在把柯西不等式拆开解释：核心就是 \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\)。 This method is essentially an expanded explanation of Cauchy's inequality; the core is \(a^2+b^2\ge2ab\).

七、最终答案7. Final Answer

\[ \boxed{\frac{\sqrt2}{2}} \]
最终答案：\(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\)，当 \(a=b\) 时取得。Final answer: \(\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\), achieved when \(a=b\).
