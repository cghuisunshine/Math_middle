若 \(m^2-m+1=0\)，求 \(m^{2024}-m^{2023}\)。If \(m^2-m+1=0\), find \(m^{2024}-m^{2023}\).
\[ m^2-m+1=0,\qquad \text{求 }m^{2024}-m^{2023}. \]

三、解法一：从 \(m^2=m-1\) 自然推出周期3. Method 1: Naturally Derive the Cycle from \(m^2=m-1\)

由已知：From the given equation:

\[ m^2-m+1=0 \]

先移项：Rearrange it first:

\[ m^2=m-1 \]

两边乘以 \(m\)：Multiply both sides by \(m\):

\[ m^3=m(m-1)=m^2-m \]

而由原式 \(m^2-m+1=0\)，有：But from \(m^2-m+1=0\), we have:

\[ m^2-m=-1 \]

所以：Therefore:

\[ m^3=-1 \]

进一步得到：Then:

\[ m^6=1 \]

也就是说，\(m\) 的幂每 6 次循环一次。So the powers of \(m\) repeat every 6 steps.

\[ 2024\equiv2\pmod6,\qquad 2023\equiv1\pmod6 \]
\[ m^{2024}-m^{2023}=m^2-m \]

再由原式：Again from the original equation:

\[ m^2-m=-1 \]
答案：\(-1\)Answer: \(-1\)

四、解法二：提取公因式4. Method 2: Factor First

\[ m^{2024}-m^{2023}=m^{2023}(m-1) \]

由 \(m^2-m+1=0\)，得：From \(m^2-m+1=0\), we get:

\[ m^2=m-1 \]

所以：So:

\[ m-1=m^2 \]
\[ m^{2024}-m^{2023}=m^{2023}\cdot m^2=m^{2025} \]

因为 \(2025=3\times675\)，并且 \(m^3=-1\)，所以：Since \(2025=3\times675\), and \(m^3=-1\):

\[ m^{2025}=(m^3)^{675}=(-1)^{675}=-1 \]
