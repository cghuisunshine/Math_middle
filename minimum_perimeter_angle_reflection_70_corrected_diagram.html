一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：∠AOB=55°，P 为角内定点，M、N 分别在 OA、OB 上运动；当 △PMN 周长最小时，求 ∠MPN。 Original image: ∠AOB=55°. P is fixed inside the angle. M and N move on OA and OB. When the perimeter of △PMN is minimized, find ∠MPN.
∠AOB = 55° P 为角内定点 M ∈ OA，N ∈ OB 当 △PMN 周长最小时，求 ∠MPN

二、讲解图：反射展开2. Diagram: Reflection Unfolding

三、解法：反射法3. Solution: Reflection Method

把点 P 关于 OA 反射到 P₁，关于 OB 反射到 P₂。Reflect point P across OA to P₁, and across OB to P₂.

因为 M 在 OA 上，所以：Since M lies on OA:

PM = P₁M

因为 N 在 OB 上，所以：Since N lies on OB:

PN = P₂N

于是 △PMN 的周长：Thus the perimeter of △PMN is:

PM + MN + PN = P₁M + MN + NP₂

要让这个和最小，就是让 P₁、M、N、P₂ 在一条直线上。To minimize this sum, P₁, M, N, and P₂ should lie on one straight line.

四、求 ∠MPN4. Find ∠MPN

设直线 MN 与 OA、OB 所成的两个角分别为 α、β。由于 OA 与 OB 的夹角是 55°，所以：Let the angles that line MN makes with OA and OB be α and β. Since the angle between OA and OB is 55°:

α + β = 55°

反射后，入射角等于反射角，因此在三角形 PMN 中：By reflection, the angle of incidence equals the angle of reflection. Therefore in triangle PMN:

∠PMN = 2α ∠PNM = 2β

所以：So:

∠PMN + ∠PNM = 2α + 2β = 2(α+β) = 110°

三角形内角和为 180°：The angles of a triangle sum to 180°:

∠MPN = 180° - 110° = 70°
答案：∠MPN = 70°Answer: ∠MPN = 70°

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

∠MPN = 70°
最终答案：70°Final answer: 70°
