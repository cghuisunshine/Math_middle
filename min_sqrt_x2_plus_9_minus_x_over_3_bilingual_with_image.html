三、解法一：设原式等于 \(k\)，根据二次方程有实数解求最小值3. Method 1: Let the Expression Equal \(k\), Then Use the Existence of Real Roots

设原式等于 \(k\)：

Let the expression be equal to \(k\):

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9}-\frac13x=k \]

移项得：

Move the linear term to the other side:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9}=k+\frac13x \]

两边平方：

Square both sides:

\[ x^2+9=\left(k+\frac13x\right)^2 \]
\[ x^2+9=k^2+\frac{2k}{3}x+\frac19x^2 \]

整理成关于 \(x\) 的二次方程：

Rearrange into a quadratic equation in \(x\):

\[ \frac89x^2-\frac{2k}{3}x+(9-k^2)=0 \]
\[ 8x^2-6kx+81-9k^2=0 \]

因为这个方程要有实数解，所以判别式必须满足 \(\Delta\ge0\)：

For this quadratic to have a real solution, its discriminant must satisfy \(\Delta\ge0\):

\[ (-6k)^2-4\cdot 8\cdot(81-9k^2)\ge0 \]
\[ 36k^2-32(81-9k^2)\ge0 \]
\[ 324k^2-2592\ge0 \]
\[ k^2\ge8 \]

又因为原式恒为正，所以只取正值：

Since the original expression is always positive, we only take the positive case:

\[ k\ge2\sqrt2 \]
因此原式的最小值为 \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\)。 Therefore, the minimum value is \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\).

四、解法二：设最小值为 \(k\)，反推柯西不等式构造4. Method 2: Let the Minimum Be \(k\), Then Reverse-Engineer a Cauchy Construction

我们希望证明：

We want to prove:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9}-\frac13x\ge k \]

等价于：

This is equivalent to:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9}\ge k+\frac13x \]

注意到：

Notice that:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9}=\sqrt{x^2+3^2} \]

它可以看成向量 \((x,3)\) 的长度。根据柯西不等式（点积形式），若 \(u^2+v^2=1\)，则

This is the length of the vector \((x,3)\). By Cauchy’s inequality (dot-product form), if \(u^2+v^2=1\), then

\[ \sqrt{x^2+3^2}\ge ux+3v \]

为了让右边恰好等于 \(\frac13x+k\)，我们令：

To make the right-hand side exactly \(\frac13x+k\), let:

\[ u=\frac13,\qquad 3v=k \]
\[ v=\frac{k}{3} \]

又因为必须满足 \(u^2+v^2=1\)，所以：

Since we must have \(u^2+v^2=1\), we get:

\[ \left(\frac13\right)^2+\left(\frac{k}{3}\right)^2=1 \]
\[ \frac19+\frac{k^2}{9}=1 \]
\[ k^2=8 \]
\[ k=2\sqrt2 \]

于是得到：

Thus we obtain:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9}\ge \frac13x+2\sqrt2 \]

两边减去 \(\frac13x\)：

Subtract \(\frac13x\) from both sides:

\[ \sqrt{x^2+9}-\frac13x\ge2\sqrt2 \]
所以原式的最小值为 \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\)。这个方法的关键是：先设最小值为 \(k\)，再反推柯西不等式中所需的系数。 So the minimum value is \(\boxed{2\sqrt2}\). The key idea is: first let the minimum be \(k\), then reverse-engineer the coefficients needed in Cauchy’s inequality.
